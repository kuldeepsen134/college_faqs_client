MBA in Banking & Finance

MBA in Banking & Finance deals with financial management, corporate finance, and aspects of international finance.  The course offers an in-depth study of banking management along with insights into finance and operation management. 

MBA in Banking & Finance course provides a strong foundation in accounting and finance particularly. This course is mainly used for those who wish to pursue a professional accountancy qualification or an academic study in related areas after graduation. The course covers major theory and practical’s related to financial accounting, working capital management, Mergers and acquisitions etc.

FAQs – MBA in Banking & Finance

 

Q. What is the eligibility to pursue MBA in Banking & Finance?

Ans: Anyone with a valid graduation degree in any discipline with minimum 50 per cent marks is eiligible to pursue MBA / PGDM in Banking & Finance. For reserved category students, the minimum mark is 45 per cent.

 

Q. Can I pursue an MBA in Banking & Finance course online?

Ans. Yes, MBA in Banking & Finance course can be pursued in online mode. There are universities offering it in Part-time and distance mode also.

 

Q. What is the average cost of admission to MBA in Banking & Finance?

Ans. The average cost of pursuing MBA in Banking & Finance from a government college is Rs. 1 Lac, whereas the fee of a private college is in the range of Rs. 8 to Rs. 15 Lac.

 

Q. What is the difference between MBA in Finance and MBA in Banking & Finance?

Ans. MBA in Finance focuses on the core finance and allied services industry. The program is based on financial theory & application with focus on finance, fintech, and financial services industry. MBA in Banking & Finance focuses on the growing needs of management professionals for Banking and Financial Services industry. The program is focused to develop quality management professionals equipped with technical and managerial competency.

Q. What are some of the top jobs for MBA in Banking & Finance graduates?

Ans. MBA / PGDM in Banking & Finance provides comprehensive career scope to a candidate with job options in various industries like market research, banking, financial services, non-banking, teaching, etc. Some of the top jobs available in government and private sector for such candidates are Bank and Finance Managers, Accountants, Auditors, Financial Advisor, Banking Loan Officers etc.

 

Q. What is the average salary of a fresher after completing MBA / PGDM in Banking & Finance?

Ans: The average salary of MBA / PGDM in Banking & Finance fresher ranges between Rs. 5 Lac to Rs. 8 Lac per annum.

 

Q: What is the admission process for MBA / PGDM in Banking & Finance course?

Ans: Admission process is mostly dependent on the B-school a student is targeting. In general the admission processed comprises of entrance test followed up by group discussion and personal interview.
