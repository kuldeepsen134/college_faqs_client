MBA in Aviation Management

MBA in Aviation Management prepare students with the knowledge to manage operations, expenses, resource management of any aviation firm or airline. The areas of focus are Aviation Business, fundamentals of General Management and additional specialization in International Business, Marketing, and Finance. Students with an MBA in Aviation Management have diverse job opportunities in fuel companies, aerospace, regulating authorities, and much more.

MBA in Aviation Management has a broader job scope with different job designations, including airport manager, airport operation management, airport planner, airport director, and many more.

Q: What is the eligibility criterion for MBA in Aviation Management?

Ans: For MBA in Aviation Management candidates must have completed graduation from a recognised university in any discipline with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks. For reserved category students the minimum marks required is 45%.
