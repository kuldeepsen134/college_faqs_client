MBA in Digital Marketing

MBA in Digital Marketing is a specialist course for students interested in learning about the various components of the digital marketing field. It is concerned with the growth of various brands or items via various digital media platforms. However, it is to be noted Digital Marketing is taught as electives also in some colleges as a part of marketing specialisation.

The career scope of Digital Marketing is attracting people to enhance their skills and get future-ready. Several institutes have been offering Digital Marketing courses via degree, diploma or certificate programmes. The Digital Marketing courses are delivered in different modes including online, distance, part-time, full-time, etc.

FAQs – MBA in Digital Marketing

 

Q: Who should opt for a Digital Marketing course?

Ans: Anybody who is more interested in online and digital medium of marketing should go for a Digital Marketing course instead of a regular Marketing degree. Companies are also now hiring digital marketing professionals.

 

Q: What is the eligibility criterion for MBA in Digital Marketing?

Ans: Digital Marketing degree course offered at UG level requires the candidates to have Class 12 level qualification, irrespective of the stream. Whereas, a PG degree in Digital Marketing requires one to have completed a bachelor's degree in any discipline with minimum 50 per cent marks. For reserved category students, minimum marks requirement is 45 per cent.

Q: What is the duration of a Digital Marketing course?

Ans: Digital Marketing course is available in full time, part time, online, distance and certificate based modes. So the duration of the course is totally dependent on the mode of course a student is opting for. Candidates pursuing MBA / PGDM in Digital Marketing can complete the course in a minimum of two years.

 

Q: What are the job prospects after completing a Digital Marketing course?

Ans: With companies moving into digital marketing, demand for such professionals is also increasing. Job roles offered to MBA / PGDM in Digital Marketing graduates include SEO Manager, Digital Marketing Manager, Social Media Manager, Content Writer, Paid Media Manager, Campaign Manager, etc.

 

Q: What are some top colleges in India to pursue a Digital Marketing course?

Ans: Some of the top colleges offering MBA / PGDM in Digital Marketing are IIM’s, Great Lakes Institute of Management, Chennai, Institute of Engineering and Management, Kolkata, Jagdish Sheth School of Management, Bangalore, ITM Business School, Navi Mumbai. To find out about the top colleges of India offering MBA / PGDM in Digital Marketing, click here.

 

 Q: Do I need to appear for any entrance exams for Digital Marketing admission?

Ans: For MBA / PGDM in Digital Marketing a candidate need to appear for entrance exams such as CAT, XAT, NMAT, SNAP, GMAT, MAT, CMAT etc. For BBA (Digital Marketing) admission is typically based on Class 12 academic merit, however, some colleges conduct internal entrance exams for admission.

 

Q: Is an online Digital Marketing course better than a full-time Digital Marketing course?

Ans: Time duration of the course also emphasizes the content part of the Digital Marketing course. Since Digital Marketing requires more practical knowledge than theoretical knowledge, some online Digital Marketing courses are better than full-time courses especially for learning specific subjects. However, with a full-time Digital Marketing course, one might learn a lot more than an online course.

 

Q: Which companies hire Digital Marketing professionals?

A: Some of the top companies that hire digital marketing experts are Google, Facebook, Amazon, iProspect India, Webchutney, Mirum India, Quasar Media, Pinstorm, etc.

Q: Can I pursue an online Digital Marketing course after Class 10?

A: Yes, several online Digital Marketing certificate courses are available which do have any minimum qualification criterion. Interested candidates can enroll themselves into such certificate courses.

 

Q. What is the average pay for an MBA in Digital Marketing?

Ans: For a fresher, pay scale for an MBA / PGDM in Digital Marketing can start in the range of Rs. 5 – 6 Lac per annum.  However, with work experience and knowledge in a period of 3 – 5 years it can increase to Rs. 10 – 15 Lac per annum.

 

Q. Is an internship a compulsory part of the MBA in Digital Marketing program?

Ans: Yes, internship a compulsory part of the MBA / PGDM in Digital Marketing full time program.
