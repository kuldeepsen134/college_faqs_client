MBA in Agri-business Management

Agribusiness is the business sector encompassing farming and farming-related commercial activities. The business involves all the steps required to send an agricultural good to market. It is an important component of the economy in countries with cultivatable land and is also important for rural economy.

The essential subjects include agribusiness management and global food supply chain management. This course is best suited for students aspiring to pursue a career in agriculture and its allied fields. Job scopes are diverse and are primarily offered in food production companies, the farming industry, and the marketing industry.

FAQs – MBA in Agri-business Management

 

Q: What is Agriculture Business Management course?

Ans: Agricullture Business Management or ABM refers to the collective business activities in food, fiber and agricultural industry. The course can be pursued at both under-graduate and post -graduate levels.

 

Q: What will I learn in the agriculture and food business course?

Ans: The course accustoms the students about the various intersections of business, food industry and agriculture. It also teaches the students how to use economic concepts along with management tools to effectively identify, analyse, and solve problems agriculture related problems .

Q: Can I pursue MBA in Agriculture and Food Business in distance or online mode?

Ans: Yes, MBA in Agri-business Management is provided by some top colleges in distance and online mode.

 

Q: What are the job profiles available after completing MBA in agri-business management?

Ans: Candidates who wish to pursue a career in agri-business can opt for profiles like Commodity Merchandiser, Farm Appraiser, Marketing Head, Agriculture Policy Analyst, Quality Controller, Rural Manager, Sourcing Specialist, etc.

Ans: Companies such as Tata, Godrej Agrovet, Pioneering Ventures, KPMG, Amazon, Dhaanya Seeds, etc. hire graduates who have studied food and agriculture business.
