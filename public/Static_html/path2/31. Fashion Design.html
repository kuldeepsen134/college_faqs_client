MBA in Fashion Design Management

With economic growth, average income of Indians’ has increased. As a result, people with more disposable income can afford to spend more on their clothing. This has made Fashion Designing very popular in India over the last few years, and many people now consider it a career option.

MBA in Fashion Designing deals with growing and sustaining the brand image and recognition of the product through media and advertising. This course is best suited for candidates with knowledge of fashion industry and business. Fashion Designing graduates have job opportunities in the field of fashion designing, multimedia, clothing-line business, product management, and much more that restricting to media and entertainment.

FAQs – MBA in Fashion Design Management

 

Q: Who should pursue a Fashion Design Management course?

Ans: MBA in Fashion Design course is best suited for candidates with knowledge of fashion industry. Fashion Designing graduates have job opportunities in the field of fashion designing, multimedia, clothing-line business, product management, and much more that restricting to media and entertainment.

 

 

Q: What are the key skills taught in MBA in Fashion Design Management?

Ans: Major components of study and skill development included with Fashion Design discipline are project accounting, design concepts focused on the user, creativity for transcending traditional ideologies, project management, operations management, art of bringing creativity into brands/ organizations, concepts of Interior Design.

 

Q: What is the eligibility criterion for MBA in Fashion Design Management?

Ans: The minimum eligibility criterion for MBA / PGDM in Fashion Design Management is graduation in any stream or equivalent from a recognised university with minimum 50 per cent aggregate marks (45 per cent for reserved categories).

 

Q: What is the syllabus of MBA in Fashion Design Management?

Ans: In four semesters of MBA in Fashion Design Management students are taught Indian Fashion Scenario, Basic Concepts of Fashion Products and Manufacturing, Global Fashion Scenario, Apparel Production Planning and Control, e- Business and e-Commerce, Quality Management, Principles of Fashion Marketing and Merchandising, Portfolio Presentation and CAD, International Trade Operation and Documentation, Apparel Export Management and Merchandising, Elements of Textile, Competition and Strategies in Fashion.

 

Q: Which colleges in India offer MBA in Fashion Design Management course?

Ans: Top colleges in India offering MBA in Fashion Design Management are IIFT’s, MIT Institute of Design - Pune, AIBM- Faridabad, Cadence Academy of Design - Nagpur, Cindrebay School of Interior Design - Calicut, International Institute of Fashion Design- Pune. To find out about the top colleges of India offering MBA / PGDM in Fashion Design Management, click here.

 

Q: What is the admission process of MBA in Fashion Design Management?

Ans: The admission process of MBA in Fashion Design Management is same as other MBA specialisations, i.e. a candidate has to clear the cut-off on the basis of entrance exam followed by selection rounds of personal interview and group discussion.

 

Q: What are the job roles offered after MBA in Fashion Design Management?

Ans: An MBA in Fashion Designing graduate gets numerous job opportunities in the fields of clothing, film, fashion designing, brand marketing, advertising, social media, and much more. Some of the job roles offered are Apparel Production Manager, Costume Designer, Fabric Buyer, Fabric Quality Control Manager, Showroom Sales Executive, Technical Sales Manager, Operations Manager, Product Marketing Manager.

 

Q: What is the average fee of an MBA in Fashion Design Management course in India?

Ans: The average fees of an MBA in Fashion Design Management course in India in a government institution is between Rs. 1 – 2 Lac. In private institutions, the fees range is from Rs. 4 – 15 Lac.

 

Q: Which entrance exam scores are valid for apply to an MBA in Fashion Design Management course?

Ans: For admission in MBA in Fashion Design Management course, CAT, MAT, CMAT, XAT, GMAT, and ATMA scores are valid.
