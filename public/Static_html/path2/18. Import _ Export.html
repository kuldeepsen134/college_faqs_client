MBA in Import and Export

MBA in Import & Export give foundation to a career in international markets and foreign trade. The program teaches candidates regarding product development, payments, financial processes, documentations, international business etc. With the opening up of international market, opportunities for qualified and skilled professionals to administer the import & export process have grown enormously.

Candidates after pursuing the course are inducted in global firms in domains like pricing, sourcing, costing, export market research, distribution, freight marketing, export finance, documentation and foreign exchange. Candidates find opportunities in companies like Reliance Industries, Indian Oil, Bharat Petroleum, Tata Motors etc as Export Manager, Import Manager, International Logistics Supply Manager.

MBA in Import and Export: Job Profiles Offered

Manufacturing companies, banks, financial institutions and government agencies that trade outside their countries lucratively hire such professionals. Such postgraduates are entrusted with tasks like analyzing technical data in international business deals and in spheres of international trade policies & agreements. Successful postgraduates of the course are hired in capacities such as:

·         Foreign Trade Manager

·         Export Manager

·         Relationship Manager

·         Foreign Trade Analyst

·         Foreign Trade and Purchasing Specialist

FAQs – MBA in Import & Export

 

Q. What is the career scope after MBA / PGDM in Import & Export?

Ans: Candidates after pursuing this course are inducted in global firms in domains like pricing, sourcing, costing, export market research, distribution, freight marketing, export finance, documentation and foreign exchange. Candidates find opportunities in companies like Reliance Industries, Indian Oil, Bharat Petroleum, Tata Motors etc as Export Manager, Import Manager, and International Logistics Supply Manager, etc.

 

Q.  Who is eligible to pursue MBA / PGDM in Import & Export?

Ans: Anyone with a legitimate graduation degree in any discipline with minimum 50 per cent marks is eiligible to pursue MBA / PGDM in Import & Export. For reserved category students, the minimum mark is 45 per cent.

 

Q. What is the course fee of MBA / PGDM in Import & Export?

Ans: MBA / PGDM in Import & Export is offered as a specialization in few B-schools. The course fee for entire 2 years ranges from Rs. 2 Lac to Rs. 20 Lac.

 

Q. What is the average salary of a fresher after completing MBA / PGDM in Import & Export?

Ans: The average salary of a fresher after graduating from MBA / PGDM in Import & Export ranges between Rs. 4 – 5 Lac.

 

Q: MBA in International Business or MBA in Import & Export, which specialisation has more scope?

Ans: Both International Business and Import & Export are very popular and have a lot of scope and both the specialization has some similarities also. The candidate must choose either of the two specializations or any other specializations of their choice based on their skills, interest and future goal.
