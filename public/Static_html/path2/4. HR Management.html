MBA in Human Resource (HR)

Human Resource Management is defined as the process involved in a recruiting workforce, provisions of their growth and training, creation and maintenance of workforce regulation, evaluation of their results, provision of compensation & benefits, encouragement, labour relations, health, welfare, and safety assessment.

Managing and retaining the best human resources at the work place has become a challenge and therefore the demand for candidates who have done MBA / PGDM in HR is growing. Human resources specialists are in demand today for deploying their skills to hire, manage and retain talent in these dynamic & challenging times.

FAQs – MBA in Human Resource (HR)

Q. What are the career scope or employment opportunities after MBA / PGDM in Human Resource (HR)?

Ans: Government, non-government, small-scale, large-scale or a start-up, Human Resources is a very critical department of any organisation. Thus, employment opportunities are very high in this field. After completing MBA in HR one can get managerial role in the Human Resources department of organisations in any sector of choice.

 

Q.  Who is eligible to pursue MBA / PGDM in Human Resource (HR)?

Ans: Anyone with a legitimate graduation degree in any discipline with minimum 50 per cent marks is eiligible to pursue MBA / PGDM in Human Resource (HR). For reserved category students, the minimum mark is 45 per cent.

 

Q. What are the major responsibilities of HR specialists?

Ans. An HR Specialist's job responsibilities include recruiting, training, managing payroll, talent acquisition, coordination with employees, etc.

 

Q: Can I pursue an MBA / PGDM in HR in part-time mode?

Ans: Yes, MBA / PGDM in HR can be pursued in part-time mode and there are options also for distance learning,  online education mode and executive MBA

 

Q. What are the skills needed for MBA / PGDM in HR?

Ans. Candidates aspiring to pursue MBA / PGDM in HR must be equipped with Strategic Thinking, Planning Skills, Leadership Skills, Communication Skills, Problem Solving Skills, Coaching Skills etc.

 

Q. What is the course fee of MBA / PGDM in Human Resource (HR)?

Ans: MBA / PGDM in Human Resource (HR) is offered as a specialization in B-schools. The course fee for entire 2 years ranges from Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 25 Lac.

 

Q. What is the average salary of a fresher after completing MBA / PGDM in Human Resource (HR)?

Ans: The average salary of MBA / PGDM in HR ranges between Rs. 4 Lac to Rs. 15 Lac per annum.

 

Q: What is the admission process for MBA / PGDM in Human Resource (HR) course?

Ans: Admission process is mostly dependent on the B-school a student is targeting. In general the admission processed comprises of entrance test followed up by group discussion and personal interview.

 

Q. Which is the best college for MBA / PGDM in HR in India?

Ans. MBA / PGDM in HR as a course is offered in various B-schools across India. Xaviers Institute of Management (XLRI), Jamshedpur is the pioneer of Human Resource Management course in India.

 

Q: Which are the top colleges for MBA / PGDM in HR in India?

Ans: Top colleges in India for MBA / PGDM in HR include Xaviers Institute of Management (XLRI), Jamshedpur, S P Jain Institute of Management and Research (SPJIMR), Mumbai, Institute of Management Technology (IMT), Ghaziabad, Jamnalal Bajaj Institute of Management Studies (JBIMS), Mumbai, K J Somaiya Institute of Management Studies & Research, Mumbai, (more names of our  tie up colleges to be written)

 

Q: What do you study in MBA / PGDM in Human Resource (HR)?

Ans: MBA / PGDM in HR syllabus includes subjects such as Human Resource Management , Management Process and Organization Theory, Fundamentals of Industrial Relations, Economics of Human Resources, Managing Interpersonal and Group Processes, Strategic Human Resource and Management, Labour Laws, Human Capital Management and HR Audit, etc.

 

Q: Which are the top companies who hire for MBA / PGDM in Human Resource (HR)?

Ans: Every company whether big or small require HR professionals. Some top companies that hire MBA / PGDM in HR are Reliance, Infosys, Tech Mahindra, Pepsico, Wipro, KPMG, Larsen & Turbo, L’Oreal, etc.

 

Q: Which are the top entrance exams for MBA / PGDM in Human Resource (HR)?

Ans: The top entrance exams for MBA in Human Resource (HR) are CAT, XAT, SNAP, NMAT, MAT, CMAT, and GMAT.
