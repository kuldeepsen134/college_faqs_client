MBA in Textile Management

MBA / PGDM in Textile Management is one of the niche specialisations and is opted by aspirants having a creative mind. MBA in Textile Management is ideal for students who have done Fashion Designing or Textile Designing in graduation.

The program has been designed to offer to eligible students, comprehensive learning in managerial aspects of textile designing in the fashion sector, sales and marketing-related management of textiles, garment production management skills, etc.

MBA in Textile Management: Job Profiles Offered

Textile Industry is considered as the second largest employer after agriculture in India hence candidates have excellent growth opportunities. There is involvement of creativity as well as technology in this field. The job roles in this field come with good remuneration and perks. Some of the job profiles to a Textile Management graduate are:

·         Production Assistant

·         Quality Control Supervisor

·         Merchandiser

·         Logistics Manager

·         Technical Sales Manager

·         Operations Manager

·         Product Marketing Manager

FAQs – MBA in Textile Management

 

Q: Who should pursue a Textile Management course?

Ans: Textile Management course is ideal for Fashion Design or Textile Design graduates. Individuals who wish to make a career in textile industry or wish to set up their own textile brand must pursue this Textile Management course.

 

Q: What are the key skills taught in MBA in Textile Management?

Ans: Apart from the core business management and organizational skills, the program has been designed to offer to eligible students, comprehensive learning in managerial aspects of textile designing in the fashion sector, sales and marketing-related management of textiles, garment production management skills, etc.

 

Q: What is the eligibility criterion for MBA in Textile Management?

Ans: The minimum eligibility criterion for MBA / PGDM in Textile Management is graduation in any stream or equivalent from a recognised university with minimum 50 per cent aggregate marks (45 per cent for reserved categories).

 

Q: What is the syllabus of MBA in Textile Management?

Ans: In four semesters of MBA in Textile Management students are taught Basics of Textiles, Fashion Merchandizing, Strategic Management, Apparel Quality Standards and Implementation, Entrepreneurship Management, Yarn and Fabric Production Management, Garment production Management, Textiles Logistics and Supply Chain Management, Research Methodology, Textile Costing, Advertising and Sales Promotion and Project Management.

 

Q: Which colleges in India offer Textile Management courses?

Ans: There are a very few colleges in India that offer Textile Management courses. Some of the top colleges offering it are IIFT’s, Delhi Institute of Fashion and Technology, New Delhi, Pacific Institute of Fashion Technology, Udaipur, Institute of Business Management Studies, Mumbai, National Institute of Retail Management,    Bangalore, Athos College of Management, Pune. To find out about the top colleges of India offering MBA / PGDM in Textile Management, click here.

 

Q: What is the admission process of MBA in Textile Management?

Ans: The admission process of MBA in Textile Management is same as other MBA specialisations, i.e. a candidate ahs to clear the cut-off on the basis of entrance exam followed by selection rounds of personal interview and group discussion.

 

Q: What are the job roles offered after MBA in Textile Management?

Ans: Some of the job roles offered after completing MBA in Textile Management include Technical Sales Manager, Production Assistant, Product Marketing Manager, Operations Manager, Logistic Manager, Quality Control Supervisor, Merchandiser etc.

 

Q: Can I pursue MBA in Textile Management in online mode?

Ans: Yes, one can pursue MBA in Textile Management in online mode. For working professionals part time and distance mode are also available.

 

 

Q: What is the average fee of an MBA in Textile Management course in India?

Ans: The average fees of an MBA in Textile Management course in India in a government institution is between Rs. 1 – 5 Lac. In private institutions, the fees range is from Rs. 6 – 12 Lac.

 

Q: Which entrance exam scores are valid for apply to an MBA in Textile Management course?

Ans: For admission to MBA in Textile Management course, CAT, MAT, CMAT, XAT, NMAT, GMAT, SNAP and ATMA scores are valid.
