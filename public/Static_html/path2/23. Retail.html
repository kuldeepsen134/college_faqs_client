MBA in Retail Management

MBA in Retail Management includes all the various steps and processes which help the customers to procure the desired merchandise from the retail stores and satisfy their shopping needs.  The retail sector is nowadays a booming industry and is creating ample job opportunities for those who have an avid interest in sales-marketing, business diversification, campaigning, advertising, market research and segmentation.

MBA in Retail Management course offers insight into the fundamentals and strategies of the functional areas of retail industry. Students are taught to find challenges, think creatively, and develop new ways to develop and improve the retail process.

FAQs – MBA in Retail Management

 

Q: What is Retail Management?

Ans: Retail Management is the process of running and managing a retail store and its day to day activities surrounding selling the goods and services to consumers.

 

Q: What is the scope of Retail Management?

Ans: MBA / PGDM in Retail Management offers scope starting career as a sales executives and achieve a higher position as a mall manager or store manager after the experience is gained.  MBA in Retail Management candidates are also hired in Educational Departments, Ration Stores, Shipping companies, Banks etc.

Q: What is the eligibility criterion for MBA in Retail Management?

Ans: For MBA in Retail Management candidates must have completed graduation from a recognised university in any discipline with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks For reserved category students the minimum marks required is 45%.

 

Q. What is the average fee to pursue MBA in Retail Management?

Ans: Fees is totally dependent upon the college and also upon the college being a government college or a private college. Fees range for MBA in Retail Management program is Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 15 Lac.

 

Q: What is the average salary offered after a Retail programme?

Ans: Freshers in Retail Management are offered an annual salary between the range of Rs. 4 Lac to Rs. 5 Lac. As they climb up the professional ladder in their career, their compensation may increase.

 

Q: Which are the top entrance exams in India to pursue MBA in Retail Management?

Ans: Entrance exams such as CAT, XAT, MAT, CMAT, etc. are accepted by some of the top Retail B-schools in India.

 

Q: What are the career options after pursuing MBA in Retail Management?

Ans: MBA / PGDM in Retail Management graduates can opt for jobs as Merchandising Manager, Retail Manager, Store Manager, Analyst, Supply Chain Manager, etc.

 

Q: Which are the top recruiters which hire Retail graduates?

A: Top companies hiring MBA in Retail Management students are DMart, Adidas Group, V-mart Retail Ltd., Shoppers Stop, Aditya Birla Fashion & Retail Ltd, Reliance Retail Ltd., Walmart, Carrefour, IKEA, Spar International, ITC, TESCO, etc.

 

Q: What is the average salary of a Retail Manager in India?

A: The average salary of a Retail Manager in India is approximately Rs. 6 Lacs.

 

Q. Is it necessary to have an UG degree in the retail management domain to pursue MBA in Retail Management?

Ans. No, it is not to have UG degree in retail to pursue MBA in Retail Management. A graduate from any discipline with required marks can pursue it.
