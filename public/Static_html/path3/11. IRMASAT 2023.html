About IRMASAT Examination

 

IRMASAT or IRMA Social Awareness Test is conducted by the Institute of Rural Management Anand (IRMA). IRMASAT scores are considered for admission to Post Graduate Diploma in Rural Management (PGDRM) and Fellow Programme in Rural Management (FPRM).

Admission to IRMA’s PGDRM course is done on the basis of CAT / XAT / GMAT scores. The admission process includes – Application submission, Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI). The IRMASAT 2023 application form will be available online on the official website of IRMA.

IRMASAT 2023: Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility Criteria for Domestic Candidates:

·         Candidate must be a graduate in any discipline from a university that is state or centrally recognised.

·         Candidates must have scored at least 50 per cent aggregate or equivalent CGPA. This aggregate is 45 per cent for SC/ ST/ DAP.

·         Candidates in their final year of the examination are also eligible for the IRMASAT 2023. They are required to submit proof of qualifying the final year by September 2023, failing which their candidature will be cancelled.

·         Candidates must have scored 85 percentile or above in CAT 2022 or XAT 2023. These candidates will be eligible for getting shortlisted for Merit-based Calls.

·         Candidates must have scored 80 percentile or above in CAT 2022 or XAT 2023 and should fulfill the Profile-based Call criteria as mentioned above.

Eligibility Criteria for Foreign Candidates:

·         Candidate must be a Graduate in any stream with at least 50% aggregate or equivalent from a recognised university.

·         Candidate must have appeared in GMAT / GRE (from 2015 onwards) and have a score that meets the minimum cut off criteria set by IRMA.

·         Foreign candidates do not have to appear in IRMASAT, but they will be required to participate in Group Activity and Personal Interview round.

Eligibility Criteria for Sponsored Candidates:

·         The candidate must hold a Bachelor's degree, in any discipline, from a recognised university/institution.

·         The candidate should have worked with the sponsoring organization for a minimum of two years (as of July 1, 2023) as a regular employee on a full-time basis.

·         The sponsoring organization should be willing to grant uninterrupted leave to the applicant (if selected) for the period of study at IRMA.

·         The sponsoring organization should undertake to make arrangements for payment of fee and other dues of the applicant to IRMA.

·         If rejoining within three years for completing the second year of the programme to obtain a “PGDRM”, she/he must be working with the same organisation that sponsored her/him for the CRM and she/he needs to acquire a “NO OBJECTION CERTIFICATE (NOC)” from it.

·         There is no age limit to appear for IRMASAT.

 

 

IRMASAT 2023 Registrations

IRMASAT application process is an online one. Students who wish to appear for the IRMASAT 2023 exam are required to fill out the online application form and complete the registration process by paying the application fee. The IRMASAT application process includes the following steps:

·         Creating a new user profile by filling out the IRMASAT registration form with details such as name, date of birth, gender, email ID and phone number, etc.

·         Filling out the complete IRMASAT application form using your number and password.

·         Uploading the required documents in the prescribed format.

·         Paying the IRMASAT application fee via credit/debit card, internet banking, UPI or other banking wallets.

 

IRMASAT 2023: Exam Centres

IRMASAT 2023 and Written Ability Test-Personal Interviews (WAT-PI) will be conducted online and offline this year. In an official announcement, IRMA notified that both the WAT and Personal Interview (WAT-PI) will be held online as well as offline. Previously, IRMASAT was conducted only at the IRMA campus.

FAQs – IRMASAT Exam 2023

 

Q: Is CAT or XAT score mandatory to appear in IRMASAT?

Ans: Yes, CAT 2022 or XAT 2023 scores are prerequisites for domestic candidates for appearing in IRMASAT. For International/NRI/PIO candidates, GMAT and GRE scores are mandatory.

 

Q. How do I correct wrong entries after IRMASAT application submission?

Ans: You are required to follow instruction carefully and write the correct information before submission of your application online. You are given a facility to edit your entries prior to final submission. Once submit button is pressed and you have been allotted a form number you cannot edit the entries. In such a case you are advised to take a printout of the submitted application form, make corrections as required in the form and send it to us as per the address notified. We shall arrange to make changes in your form accordingly. However, you are advised to take enough precautions while submitting your application to avoid hassles.

 

Q: What is IRMASAT?

Ans: IRMASAT or IRMA Social Awareness Test is conducted by the Institute of Rural Management Anand (IRMA). IRMASAT scores are considered for admission to Post Graduate Diploma in Rural Management (PGDRM) and Fellow Programme in Rural Management (FPRM). Admission to IRMA’s PGDRM course is done on the basis of CAT / XAT / GMAT scores.

 

Q: How can I access the IRMA admission form?

Ans: IRMASAT application form is available on the official website. The forms can be submitted in online mode only. Last date to fill the form is 27 January, 2023.

 

Q. What is the eligibility for IRMASAT 2023 exam?

Ans. Any graduate candidate with 50% aggregate from a state or central university can apply for the exam. Candidates who have scored 85 percentile in CAT 2022 or XAT 2023 will be eligible to get a merit-based call and candidates who have scored 80 percentile in CAT 2022 or XAT 2023 will be eligible for profile-based calls.

 

Q. Can I fill the IRMASAT application form offline?

Ans. No, the IRMASAT application form 2023 was available online only on the official website. Last date to fill the form is 27 January, 2023.

 

Q. What is the purpose of IRMASAT 2023?

Ans. IRMASAT is conducted for admission to the PGDM programme of the Institute of Rural Management, Anand.

 

Q. Is there any marks relaxation for the reserved category student in the IRMASAT 2023 exam?

Ans: Yes, candidates belonging to reserved categories will get 5% relaxation in graduation marks.

Q. Where will the WAT-PI round be held for IRMASAT?

A. The WAT-PI round will be held online as well as offline this year in five cities, i.e. Anand, Guwahati, Kolkata, Delhi and Bangalore.

 

Q. Whether bank loans are available for the PGDM (RM-X) programme?

Ans: Educational loans would be available from various banks to applicants enrolling at IRMA for the programme. Nationalized Banks in and around the town provide student friendly education loan services for IRMA students. The education loan facilities are available from IDBI Bank, OBC Bank, Canara Bank for PGDM (RM-X) students.

 

Q. Does Institute have seats for Management Quota (MQ) in PGDM (RM-X) programme?

Ans: No, there is no such management quota for admission to this programme.

 

Q. Which score will be considered, if I have provided more than one (CAT / XAT/ GMAT) registration numbers?

Ans: Out of all the scores (CAT/XAT/ GMAT), the highest score will be considered for selection process.
