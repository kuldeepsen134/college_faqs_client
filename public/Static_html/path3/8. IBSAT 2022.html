About IBSAT Examination

 

IBS Aptitude Test or IBSAT is a national level entrance exam conducted by ICFAI Foundation for Higher Education (a deemed to be University) for admission to MBA/PGPM programmes.  IBSAT scores are accepted by nine ICFAI Business Schools (IBS), namely IBS Hyderabad, IBS Mumbai, IBS Bengaluru, IBS Pune, IBS Gurgaon, IBS Kolkata, IBS Ahmedabad, IBS Jaipur and IBS Dehradun.

IBSAT is a two-hour Computer-based test (CBT) comprising multiple-choice questions. IBSAT is a computer-based test that is generally conducted over a window of two days in December every year. Qualified candidates will have to first attend a selection briefing round and then appear for the final selection rounds, including Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) round.

IBSAT 2022 Eligibility Criteria

An IBSAT 2022 aspiring candidate has to fulfill the below-given eligibility criteria for appearing in IBSA exam 2022:

·         Graduation in any discipline with minimum 50% marks and medium of instruction as English.

·         All applicants should have completed a minimum of 15 years of regular education (on 10+2+3 or 10+2+4 basis).

·         Applicants in their final year bachelor's degree course are also eligible to apply.

 

IBSAT 2022 Application Process

IBSAT application form is available in both Online and Offline mode. The details of both the process is given below.

IBSAT 2022 Online Registration

1.       Visit the official website - https://ibsat20.ibsindia.org/

2.       Click on ‘Apply Online’ and enter the required details, i.e. personal details, address, etc.

3.       Candidates need to log in with their registration ID and Password to complete IBSAT form fill up. The details that need to be filled in are:

a.       Personal Details: Candidate’s Name, Date of Birth, Gender, Nationality and Address

b.       Academic Details: Class 10th ,12th and graduation details such as college name, school name, marks and roll numbers

c.       Documents: Photograph and signature as per specifications

4.       Candidates need to make fee payment of Rs. 1,800 to complete IBSAT registration form. The fee can be paid by using Net Banking / Credit Card / Debit Card.

5.       Once, the application form fee of IBSAT 2022 is paid, click on the 'Finish' button to complete the application process.

IBSAT 2022 Offline Registration

1.       A candidate can buy the application form along with the prospectus from any of the IBS campus/ designated marketing branches by paying Rs. 1,800 in cash.

2.       The candidate will receive a packet on which a login ID and voucher number will be printed in it.

3.       Visit the official website - https://ibsat20.ibsindia.org/ and set a new password.

4.       Use login ID and new password to fill IBSAT 2022 application form.

 

IBSAT 2022 Exam Pattern

IBSAT 2022 exam will be conducted in 4th week of December 2022. It is an online computer based test of 2 hours duration. IBSAT is a moderate difficulty level exam which consists of 140 questions divided into 4 sections. Every correct answer will obtain one mark, and there is no negative marking for incorrect answer.

IBSAT Exam Syllabus

IBSAT exam syllabus is not much different than the other national level MBA entrance exams. The details of the syllabus are given below.

Syllabus

Important Topics

Verbal Ability

Grammar, Sentence formation, vocabulary usage, idioms, phrases, one word substitution, jumbled paragraphs among others

Reading Comprehension

RC passages on various themes like Art & Culture, Business, Management, Science & Technology, Global Issues, Society & Diversity, etc.

Quantitative Ability

Arithmetic (ratio & proportion, percentage, interest, time & speed, etc), Geometry, Modern Mathematics, Number system, Algebra, Trigonometry, Series.

Data Adequacy & Data Interpretation

Data interpretation (Tables, Charts, Graphs), Problem-solving, Data sufficiency.

 

 

IBSAT 2022 Exam Preparation Tips

IBSAT is a speed-based test where students need to solve 140 questions within two hours. Keeping this in mind, students need to devise a preparation timeline to cover all essential topics from the syllabus. They also need to shortlist and refer to the best books for IBSAT preparation and mock tests available on IBSAT official website.

Some of the important tips for preparing for IBSAT 2022 are:

1.       Allot three to four months to cover the IBSAT syllabus

2.        Analyze self-preparation level through practice of IBSAT sample papers and mock tests

3.       Follow a systematic plan to complete the IBSAT syllabus

4.       Join online group for preparation and discussion.

FAQs – IBSAT Exam 2022

 

Q. When will IBSAT 2022 registration commence?

Ans. IBSAT 2022 registration began on 1st July, 2022 and application form is available.

 

Q: How to apply for IBSAT 2022?

Ans: IBSAT application forms are available on the official website of IBS India. Candidates can apply for the exam by registering on the official website. A candidate can also buy the application form along with the prospectus from any of the IBS campus/ designated marketing branches by paying Rs. 1,800 in cash.

 

Q: What is the IBS Selection Briefing?

Ans: The IBS Selection Briefings are a series of seminars to provide candidates with an insight into the programmes and processes at IBS. Selection briefings are followed by Group Discussion and Personal Interview.

 

Q: What is the exam pattern of IBSAT?

Ans: The IBSAT exam is divided into four sections - Verbal Ability, Reading Comprehension, Quantitative Ability and Data Adequacy & Data Interpretation. The total number of questions will be 140 and the duration of the test is two hours.

 

Q. What is the marking scheme of IBSAT?

Ans. The total number of questions is 140 with +1 mark for each correct answer. There is no negative marking.

Q: What is the difficulty level of IBSAT exam?

Ans: IBSAT is speed based exam as there are 140 questions with only 120 minutes of time. Amongst all the other national level MBA entrance exams, IBSAT exam is considered to be of moderate difficulty level.

 

Q. How can I prepare for the IBSAT exams?

Ans: The best way to prepare for the IBSAT exam is to first know the exam pattern, structure and syllabus. The second is to make a timeline to complete the syllabus and take up mock tests to understand individual pace.

 

Q: Is there any Mock Test for IBSAT?

Ans: Yes, the mock tests is provided for the candidates prior exam, so that candidates can get familiarize with the IBSAT test. The national level IBSAT mock tests is available on the official website.

Q: Can students in their graduation apply for IBSAT exam?

Ans:  Yes, as per the eligibility criteria of the IBSAT exam, any candidate who is in the final year of their bachelor's degree can apply for the IBSAT exam.

 

Q: Is work experience necessary criteria for the IBSAT selection process?

Ans: No. work experience is not counted in the selection process. However, during personal interview round, any work experience is given consideration.

 

Q. Are candidates called for counselling only based on IBSAT score?

Ans: No, apart from the IBSAT Exam, candidates with a valid score in GMAT, CAT, NMAT by GMAC are also called for counseling.

 

Q. What is the last date for IBSAT 2022 registration?

Ans. IBSAT registration will continue before the exam due date which will be tentatively in the third week of December 2022.

 

Q. When can I download the IBSAT 2022 admit card?

Ans. IBSAT 2022 admit card is released soon after the registration process is over.

 

Q. Is there any management quota of seats for MBA admission in ICFAI colleges?

Ans. No there is no management quota or NRI quota.

 

Q. How and when the selection process is conducted?

Ans. Selection process for all 9 campuses will be tentatively conducted in 2nd and 3rd week of February, 2023 at IBS Hyderabad. If situation does not permit to conduct process at campus, ONLINE selection process will be conducted.

 

Q. What are the programmes offered by IBS?

Ans: At IBS, students can opt for the following post graduate programmes: 2 year MBA programme IBS Hyderabad, IBS Bangalore, IBS Dehradun and IBS Jaipur and PGPM (Post Graduate Program in Management) program offered by IBS Ahmedabad, IBS Gurgaon, IBS Kolkata, IBS Mumbai and IBS Pune.
