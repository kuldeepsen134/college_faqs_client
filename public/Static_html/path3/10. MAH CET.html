About MAH CET MBA Examination

 

MAH CET, also known as MAH MBA / MMS CET is a state-level management entrance test conducted in online mode by the State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra for admission into full-time postgraduate courses like MBA (Masters in Business Administration) and MMS (Masters in Management Studies) in various top institutes across Maharashtra.

The candidates looking to opt for JBIMS, SIMSREE, K J Somaiya, PUMBA and other top MBA / MMS Colleges in Maharashtra are recommended to attend MAH CET Exam. Aspirants are suggested to check regularly to receive the latest updates of MAH CET.

MAH CET 2022 Preparation Tips

Candidates are required to follow certain techniques and strategies for preparing for MAH CET 2022 Exam. Some of the important tips to follow are:

·         MAH CET is a race against time, where the candidate is required to complete 200 questions in 150 minutes. Thus practising a lot of questions can help the candidate to reduce the average time to complete each question.

·         Mock tests will help the candidate to analyze his/her speed of doing questions in the exam. Moreover, as a result of solving the mock test, the candidate can become aware of the areas that are taking more time and thus require more practice.

·         The candidates can get a fair amount of ideas about the syllabus after solving the previous year papers. Moreover, the candidate can analyze his/her weak spots in the syllabus and the time required to solve the entire paper.

·         MAH CET 2022 Reference Books are very essential for the candidates to go through and refer from, in order to prepare well for scoring higher in the examination.

MAH MBA CET 2022 Counselling

Counselling and seat allotment process is expected to begin in the first week of November. Qualified candidates need to participate in respective counselling rounds (CAP) in online mode.

Candidates should log in to the website to get their Maharashtra CET 2022 seat allotment order. They must report to the Admission Reporting Centre (ARC) for the verification of documents and report to the allotted college to finish the admission process. To register for MAH MBA CET 2022 Counselling, follow the below steps:

·         Open the official website - cetcell.mahacet.org

·         Click on "New Candidate Registration" and fill necessary details.

·         A User ID and password will be generated.

·         Now, log in to the website to fill the Maharashtra MBA CET 2022 counselling form.

MAH MBA CET 2022 Counselling Process:

MAH MBA CET 2022 counselling which will be conducted in three stages, they are:

1.       Registration & Fee Payment - Log in to the candidate portal by using the valid credentials. They have to enter all the necessary details and upload the required documents. Then counselling fee payment has to be done as per the category which is Rs. 2,000 for General and Rs. 1,000        for Reserved category.

2.       Provisional List Announcement - The names of the shortlisted candidates are published as a provisional merit list. They can submit their grievances along with the requisite documents at the facilitation centre.

3.       Release of Final Merit List - The exam authorities have released the final merit list of MAH MBA CET 2022 counselling. Students who will be shortlisted for the CAP rounds will have to report at the Admission Reporting Centre (ARC) for the document verification process and visit the allotted institute/college to complete the admission process.

Maharashtra MBA CET 2022 Counselling Registration - Highlights

·         Candidates will be able to select a maximum of three choices of colleges.

·         Once the choice is locked, it will not be changed.

·         They need to accept the seat allotment after the seat gets allocated as per their preferences.

·         Candidates are not permitted to participate in subsequent CAP rounds.

·         MAH MBA CET 2022 Seat acceptance fee is not refundable.

FAQs – MAH CET MBA 2022 Exam

 

Q: When will MAH CET result 2022 be announced?

Ans: MAH CET result is normally announced 15-20 days after the exam. The MAH MBA CET 2022 was held on August 23, 24 and 25 and the result was announced on September 11. The result is available for download on the official website.

 

Q: What is the MAH MBA CET 2022 exam date?

Ans: MBA CET 2022 exam were held on 23, 24 and 25 August, 2022. On 26 March, 2022, the Maharashtra CET had announced MAH MBA CET 2022 exam dates as June 24, 25 and 26, however, the exam dates were revised and pushed to August on May 1.

 

Q. What is the duration of MAH MBA CET 2022?

Ans: MAH MBA CET 2022 exam duration is 150 minutes for attempting 200 questions divided into 4 sections- Logical Reasoning, Abstract Reasoning, Quantitative Aptitude and Verbal Ability & Reading Comprehension.

 

Q. How many attempts are allowed in MAH CET?

Ans: A candidate can appear for MAH CET for 3 times only.

 

Q: I don’t belong to Maharashtra state. Am I eligible for MAH CET MBA?

Ans: Yes, you are eligible for MAH-CET exam under the All India Seats category which is about 15% of the total seats. The registration, exam and counselling processes for All India Seats category students are same as that of Maharashtra State category candidates.

 

Q: Can I apply to colleges outside Maharashtra with MBA score?

Ans: MAH CET is a state level management entrance exam. Institutes outside Maharashtra do not accept MAH CET score for admission.

 

Q: Who conducts MAH MBA CET?

Ans: The MAH CET exam is conducted by State CET(Common Entrance Test) Cell - Directorate of Technical Education (DTE) Maharashtra. 350+ Maharashtra colleges participate in MAH CET counselling.

 

Q: What are the application dates for MAH CET 2022?

Ans: MAH CET MBA exam application form was released on March 17 and the last date to apply was April 7, 2022. The deadline was later extended till April 20, 2022.

 

Q: What is the MAH CET application fee? Is there any fee relaxation for non-Maharashtra candidates?

Ans: MAH CET application fee is Rs. 800 for Reserved and PwD category candidates from Maharashtra and Rs. 1,000 for students from rest of India. The fees is to be paid in online mode. There is no fee relaxation for non-Maharashtra candidates.

 

Q: What is the admission process through MAH CET?

Ans: MAH CET admission process includes the entrance exam followed by Centralised Admission Process or CAP as per the college cut-offs for allotment of seats in colleges accepting MAH CET scores for MBA / MMS / PGDM courses.

 

Q: Is MAH CET counselling MBA cut off-based?

Ans: Yes, MAH MBA CET counselling is cut off-based and process for 2022 has started after result was out on Sep 11, 2022. MAH CET counselling is conducted round-wise and candidates have to register online, participate in online counselling and confirm admission by submission of documents and final admission by making fee payment.

 

Q. Will there be GD/PI after MAH CET written exam?

Ans: Only JBIMS conducts GD, PI and other selection rounds after the written test. Rest all colleges give admission only based on the written test.

 

Q: What are the MAH MBA CET 2022 counselling dates?

Ans: MAH MBA CET 2022 counselling process dates will be announced after the declaration of exam result. However, one can expect the counselling to take place in October, 2022.

 

Q: I have completed graduation from correspondence. Am I eligible to apply for MAH CET MBA?

Ans: Yes, candidates who have completed graduation from correspondence are eligible to apply, provided they have scored 50 per cent in aggregate (45 per cent for Reserved category students) and the degree awarded is by a university recognized by the UGC.

 

Q: What is the MAH CET MBA cut off for JBIMS admission?

Ans: MAH CET cut off for JBIMS admission has been 99.98 percentile for All India category candidates for the past two years. The cut off for Maharashtra state candidates is lower for JBIMS.
