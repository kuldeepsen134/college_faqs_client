About SNAP Examination

 

Symbiosis National Aptitude Test (SNAP) is a national-level management aptitude test conducted by Symbiosis International University (Deemed) for Admission to 15 Symbiosis Institutes in India. It is a highly sought-after and competitive exam. Every year around 1 lac students from all disciplines including Engineering, Commerce, Humanities and even Law take SNAP Test to get into of the 2,500 MBA seats in Symbiosis colleges.

SNAP 2022 application fee is Rs. 1950 per test along with Symbiosis Institute Programme Registration  Fee of Rs. 1000 per programme, per institute. Candidates who clear the SNAP exam cut-off are shortlisted for the Group Exercise (GE), Personal Interaction (PI) and Written Ability Test (WAT) round conducted by SIU institutes individually in the month of February and March 2023.

SNAP 2022 Eligibility Criteria

A SNAP 2022 aspiring candidate has to fulfil the below-given eligibility criteria for appearing in SNAP exam 2022:

·         Candidate should be Graduate from any recognised University/ Institution of National Importance and must have obtained a minimum of 50% marks or equivalent grade (45% marks or equivalent grade for Scheduled Castes/ Scheduled Tribes) at graduation. 

·         Candidates appearing for final year examinations can also apply, but their admission will be subject to obtaining a minimum of 50% marks or equivalent grade (45% or equivalent grade for Scheduled Castes/ Scheduled Tribes).

·         A candidate who has completed qualifying qualification from any Foreign University must obtain an equivalence certificate from Association of Indian Universities (AIU)

Note: Academic eligibility criterion for programs like Master of Business Administration (Infrastructure Development and Management), Master of Business Administration (Energy and Environment), Master of Business Administration (Operations Management), Master of Business Administration (Data Sciences & Data Analytics) differ. A candidate is advised to check the eligibility criteria for the desired programme(s).

 

SNAP 2022 Application Process

SNAP registration cum application process is online on the official website of SNAP https://www.snaptest.org/. Given below is the process to complete the SNAP application form:

1.       Visit the SNAP 2022 website

2.       Click on New Registration

7.       Upload passport size photo and scanned signature

8.       Make payment of SNAP application fee via credit/debit card or net banking

SNAP Exam Syllabus

SNAP syllabus is divided into three sections – General English, Analytical & Logical Reasoning and Quantitative Data Interpretation & Data Sufficiency. The scoring weightage allotted to these sections is 25%, 33% and 42% respectively. Candidates should include the following sections and subjects in there preparation/

Syllabus

Important Topics

General English: Reading Comprehension, Verbal Reasoning, Verbal Ability

Reading Comprehension, Fill in the blanks, Para-jumbles, Sentence Correction, Odd one out, Vocabulary, Idioms and Synonyms and Antonyms

Analytical & Logical Reasoning

Critical Reasoning, Arrangements, Puzzles, Syllogisms, Blood Relations, Coding and Decoding, Sequences and Series

Quantitative, Data Interpretation & Data Sufficiency

Algebra, Geometry, Arithmetic, Number System , Modern Mathematics, Graphs & Charts, Venn Diagrams

 

 

SNAP 2022 Exam Preparation Tips

Candidates planning to appear for the SNAP 2022 exam should start preparing for it at least six months in advance. A good SNAP exam preparation plan should have three components:

-          Syllabus practice through books

-          SNAP mock test practice

-          Daily revision for exam day

Some of the important tips for SNAP preparation are:

-          Prepare as per the SNAP syllabus for all three sections

-          Keep giving SNAP mock tests to keep track of your preparation

-          Analyze and be aware of your strong and weak subjects

-          Choose the right book and study material for SNAP preparation

-          Prepare a SNAP preparation timetable for daily use

-          Focus on increasing speed and accuracy while solving questions, sets and mock tests

-          Solve questions of varying difficulty levels to boost confidence

-          Devise or learn shortcuts to solve Quantitative questions

-          Join online SNAP study groups

FAQs – SNAP Exam 2022

 

Q: What is the date of SNAP 2022?

Ans: SNAP 2022 exam will be conducted on three days of December 2022. The SNAP exam dates are 10, 18 and 23 December, 2022. On all three days, the exam will be conducted from 2:00 PM to 3:00 PM at designated examination centres

 

Q: What is the SNAP 2022 registration start and end date?

Ans: SNAP 2022 registration started on August 4, 2022 and will go on till November 24, 2022. Candidates will be able to fill the online application form for the entrance test in this duration by visiting the official website of SNAP.

 

Q: What is the application fee for SNAP Test?

Ans: The SNAP application fee is Rs. 1,950 to be paid in online mode only through credit/debit card or internet banking for successful submission of the form. The application fee is non-refundable.

 

Q. Do I need to pay an institution registration fee for SNAP?

Ans. Yes, the shortlisted candidates have to pay the institution registration fee of Rs. 1,000 for every program, at every institution they apply for.

 

Q: What is the admission process through SNAP 2022 exam?

Ans: The admission process includes SNAP Test, Group Exercise (GE), Personal Interaction (PI), and Writing Aptitude Test (WAT). Final admission is granted to candidates based on SNAP scores and performance in GE, PI and WAT basis the weightage in each section.

 

Q. How many colleges accept SNAP score for admission?

Ans: SNAP score is accepted by 15 institutes of Symbiosis International University including SIBM, Pune.

 

Q. How many MBA seats are under SIU for MBA?

Ans: SNAP is conducted by SIU for admission into 2,500 MBA seats combining all campuses.

 

Q: How many times can I appear in SNAP exam?

Ans: SNAP is held only once a year. A candidate can appear for a maximum of two tests for SNAP 2022. If a candidate appears for three tests, the higher score will be considered for final percentile calculation and there will be no normalization.

 

Q: Is there any reservation policy in SNAP?

Ans: Symbiosis International University (SIU) does not offer any reservation. It follows the sanctioned intake under the government approved reservation policy -

·         Scheduled Caste: 15 per cent

·         Scheduled Tribe: 7.5 per cent

·         Differently Abled: 8 per cent

·         Wards of Defence Personnel: 5 per cent (applicable to selective programmes)

 

Q: What is the syllabus of SNAP 2022?

Ans: SNAP syllabus includes all the important topics of General English, Analytical & Logical Reasoning and Quantitative, Data Interpretation & Data Sufficiency.

 

Q: What is the schedule of SNAP WAT-GE-PI?

Ans: The GE-PI-WAT round of admission through SNAP will be held in February 2023 and final merit list will be out by March 2023. Final schedule will be announced by the participating institutes after SNAP 2022 result declaration.

 

Q: Is there any change in the exam pattern of SNAP 2022?

Ans: No, there is no change in the SNAP exam pattern 2022. It will be one hour examination of total 60 marks. The question paper will be based on old pattern, i.e. - General English (15 questions), Analytical & Logical Reasoning (25 questions) and Quantitative, Data Interpretation and Data Sufficiency (20 questions). 

 

Q: Is there any negative marking in SNAP?

Ans: As per the exam pattern of SNAP 2022, each correct answer fetches +1 mark and -0.25 mark for each wrong answer. Total mark of SNAP 2022 is 60.

 

Q: When will SNAP 2022 result be announced?

Ans: SNAP 2022 result will be announced on January 10, 2023. Candidates are required to download their scorecards from the exam website by using their login credentials.

 

Q: Is there any age limit to appear for SNAP?

Ans: No, there is no age limit to appear for SNAP. However, the candidate must have completed Graduation or must be in the final year of their Bachelor's degree. Working professionals are also eligible to appear for the test.

 

Q: What is the eligibility criterion for MBA courses offered by SIU?

Ans: Any candidate graduate from any recognised University/ Institution of National Importance and has obtained a minimum of 50% marks or equivalent grade (45% marks or equivalent grade for Scheduled Castes/ Scheduled Tribes) at graduation is eligible for MBA at SIU institutes. However, academic eligibility criterion for Master of Business Administration (Infrastructure Development and Management), Master of Business Administration (Energy and Environment), Master of Business Administration (Operations Management), Master of Business Administration (Data Sciences & Data Analytics) programs differ.
