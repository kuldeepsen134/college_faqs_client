About MICAT Examination

 

MICA, Ahmedabad is India’s premier management school for Strategic Marketing and Communications. Apart from PGDM and PGDM-C, MICA also offers 1-year Post Graduate Certificate Program, Crafting Creative Communications (CCC), and the 4-year Fellow Programme in Management (FPM).

MICA Admissions Test (MICAT) is the online entrance test for MICA's Post Graduate Diploma in Management - Communications (PGDM-C) program. MICAT is a unique MBA entrance exam that involves both objective and subjective questions. Along with the common sections of the MBA entrance exams like Quantitative Ability, Verbal Ability and Reasoning, there are sections like Descriptive Language Test and Psychometric Test.

Candidates need to appear for CAT / XAT / GMAT. MICAT exam is conducted in two phases, i.e. MICAT I and MICAT II. The best scores of the candidates are considered for preparing the shortlist. The final selection will be based on the performance of candidates in CAT/GMAT/XAT, MICAT, GE and PI.

MICAT 2023 Eligibility Criteria

The eligibility criteria for MICAT 2023 is as follows:

·         Candidates should have appeared or will be appearing in CAT 2022 / XAT 2023 / GMAT (2021 onwards)

·         Candidate must also have obtained a bachelor's degree or equivalent qualification in any discipline recognised by the Association of Indian Universities (AIU)

·         Candidates appearing for final year bachelor's degree examinations are also eligible to apply

 

MICAT 2023 Application Process

MICA Ahmedabad has released the MICAT application form online at official website. Candidates can apply for MICAT 2023 by providing personal and academic details. After providing the information,  candidates need to pay an application fee. MICAT 2023 Result

MICAT 2023 Result will be declared three times, twice after the MICAT written tests and the final result after the GD & PI rounds. The MICAT-I Result will be released on December 21, 2022, and the MICAT-II result will be out on February 17, 2023.

 

MICAT result can be viewed online mode. On the basis of the written result, candidates will be called for further admission rounds. To check your MICAT result:

 

·         Log on to the official website of MICAT

·         Enter your MICA ID and Password

·         Click on Result tab

·         Enter your Roll Number, Date of Birth and Application number

·         MICAT Score Card will be displayed on the computer screen

·         Download it for further use.

FAQs – MICAT Exam 2023

 

Q: What is the pattern of the MICAT exam?

Ans: MICAT exam is slightly different from most of the MBA entrance exams. It has three different sections:

1.       Psychometric Test

2.       Section A (Verbal Ability, Quantitative Ability, General Awareness and Divergent-Convergent Thinking)

3.       Section B (Descriptive Test)

 

Q: How can I pay the MICAT registration fee?

Ans: Test takers had to pay MICAT registration fee through either net banking, or debit/credit card.

 

Q. Am I still eligible for MICAT if I haven't qualified CAT?

Ans: Candidates are eligible if they have appeared for either of the three exams: CAT, XAT and GMAT.

Q. I have a bachelor's degree in English, am I still eligible for MICAT?

Ans: Candidates are eligible if they have a bachelor's degree in any subject or have an equivalent qualification from any institution recognized by the AIU or Association of Indian Universities. Candidates who are in the final year or the last year of their bachelor\'s degree are also eligible.

 

Q.  If I gave MICAT I, then do I have to write MICAT II also, or only one exam is valid?

Ans. Better of the two MICAT 2022 Scores are considered. A candidate may choose to write both MICAT I & II.

 

Q: Which specialisations are included in PGDM- C MICA?

Ans: The programme focuses on Strategic Marketing and Communication and specialisations include Brand Management, Advertising Management, Marketing Research and Analytics, Media Management and Digital Communication Management.

 

Q: How many seats are offered by MICA through MICAT 2022?

Ans: 36 PGDM and 180 PGDM-C seats are offered by MICA, Ahmedabad through MICAT.

 

Q: In how many phases are the MICAT exam conducted?

Ans: MICAT exam is conducted in two phases. MICAT I is scheduled on December 3, 2022 and MICAT II will be held on January 28, 2023.

 

Q: What is the marking scheme of the MICAT?

Ans: There is no negative marking for the Descriptive section and Psychometric section. The sections including Verbal Ability, General Awareness, Quantitative Ability, and Data Interpretation, and Divergent and Convergent Thinking have a negative marking. 0.25 percent is deducted from the allotted marks.

 

Q: In how many test centres will the MICAT 2023 exam be conducted?

Ans: MICAT 2021-22 was conducted at multiple centres in nearly 50 test cities spread across India. MICAT 2023 is expected to repeat the centers.

 

Q: Which supporting documents do I need to carry for the MICAT exam?

Ans: Test takers need to carry MICAT admit card and along with it, they can carry Aadhaar Card/Pan Card/Voter ID Card/Passport or Driving License.

 

Q: Do I have to carry the hard copy of the MICAT admit card?

Ans: Yes. Candidates need to carry the hard copy of the MICAT admit card to the exam centre. A digital copy of the admit card will not be accepted.

Q: What is the minimum score for the candidate in the group exercise and personal interview in order to get selected for the admission?

Ans: Candidates need to score at least 3.5 in the Group exercise round and a minimum of 4 score in the personal interview in order to be considered for the final selection for admission.

 

Q: What is the basic eligibility criterion for PGDM-C admissions at MICA?

Ans: Candidates holding a bachelor's degree or equivalent educational qualification in the 10+2+3 system (irrespective of the discipline) from an AIU recognised institute are eligible for the PGDM-C programme.

 

Q: Does MICA offer scholarships?

Ans: Yes, MICA offers merit-based scholarships to candidates. Each scholarship amounts up to 25% of the annual fees of the programme.

 

Q: Do other MBA / PGDM colleges accept MICAT scores?

Ans: No, the exam is exclusively held for admission to MICA, Ahmedabad. However, candidates can use their XAT, CAT or GMAT score for admission to any other institute.
