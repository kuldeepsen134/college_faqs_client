About NMAT by GMAC Examination

 

NMAT by GMAC is a national level MBA program entrance test conducted for NMIMS University and other reputed B-Schools in India as well as abroad. Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies, Mumbai conducted NMAT until GMAC took over the authority in 2015.

NMAT is considered the most student friendly management entrance test. NMAT shares some features with GMAT as candidates are allowed to choose the preferred date during a testing window as well as self-schedule the test, time and venue, and appointment date based on the availability of seats at the preferred test centre. The national level entrance exam, NMAT, is conducted once a year during the test window of specified days. NMAT by GMAC score is based on the performance of candidates in three sections, namely, Language Skills, Quantitative Skills and Logical Reasoning.

NMAT 2022 – Salient Features and New Changes:

·         No Late Registration Fee: Last date of application registration will not be extended; hence, late registration fee will NOT be charged.

·         Two Testing Modes: Home based Online Proctored Exam mode has been added from this year. Along with centre based Computer Based Online Exam, an aspirant can choose this mode also.

·         Equal Sectional Weightage: All the 3 sections in NMAT 2022 would be caring equal weightage.

·         No Negative Marking: There will be no negative marking in NMAT 2022 question paper.

·         Re-take Attempts: Maximum of three attempts are allowed to appear in NMAT 2022 exam.

·         Rescheduling: A mandatory gap of minimum 15 days is required between two attempts.

·         Re-take Seats: Retake attempts are booked on first cum first basis and subject to seat availability on the scheduled date and slot.

 

NMAT 2022 Eligibility Criteria

The minimum eligibility criterion for NMAT 2022 exam is graduation in any stream. Detailed NMAT eligibility criteria are listed below:

·         Candidates must hold a bachelor’s degree of minimum three or four years duration or equivalent in any discipline from a recognised university

·         Candidate should not have secured less than 50 per cent aggregate in the final year of graduation

·         Students in their final year of graduation can also apply

·         Admission is granted on a provisional basis to final year admission until the graduation mark sheet and certificate of the qualifying examination are submitted

 

NMAT 2022 Registrations & Application Process

Instructions and tips by following which a candidate can register and apply for NMAT 2022 are given below:

1.       Visit the official website of NMAT.

2.       Go to the registration page and click on "Register".

3.       The new registered user page will appear. The applicants will now have to enter their personal details such as name, country, mobile number, email ID and choose a password and click on the submit button.

4.       On successful registration candidates will get a 'Registration Confirmation' mail. The candidates have to verify the email by clicking on the link provided in the mail sent to their accounts.

5.       After email verification, candidate can log into their account using the email ID (user ID) and password.

6.       Once the applicants have successfully logged in, they must go to the 'My Dashboard' page, where they will access the photo upload, registration form, payments, and receipts sections.

7.       Applicants have to click on 'add photo' and upload a pre-scanned picture as per the given size specifications.

8.       Next applicants should click on 'Access My Form' and fill in all the personal information asked, such as contact info, education, work experience, school preference.

9.       Read all the terms and conditions and click on the checkbox to agree to all terms and conditions.

10.   Once the form is submitted, a unique NMAT by GMAC registration number will appear on the screen. Applicants have to note down this number.

11.   Make the payment of NMAT form fee through the available payment gateways.

12.   After successful payment of NMAT exam form fee, an email will be sent to the applicant's registered email address along with their unique NMAT by GMAC registration number.

NMAT 2022 Exam Scheduling

Candidates can schedule the NMAT 2022 exam using the scheduling tab on the official website of NMAT by GMAC. Candidates can schedule their test anytime during scheduling window, however there should be a gap of 24 hours between scheduling and exam date. The steps to following to schedule NMAT 2022 are:

1.       Login to your NMAT account and go to the 'My Dashboard'.

2.       Go to the ‘Schedule Exam’ option that will be visible once application fee has been paid successfully.

3.       Schedule your NMAT Exam date and time using ‘Schedule Exam’ feature.

4.       Choose your test mode from either of the two:

a.       Test centre based online exam

b.       Home based remote proctored exam

5.       Download your admit card from your dashboard.

NMAT 2022 Preparation Strategy

First thing a student should do for NMAT 2022 preparation is to go through the previous year's exam analysis and focus on covering the syllabus. One of the most important parts of NMAT exam preparation is solving overall and section-wise sample papers and mock tests.

Given below are the section-wise preparation tips and strategies for NMAT 2022.

NMAT 2022 Language Skills Preparation

NMAT Language Skills section comprises 36 questions from Reading Comprehension, para jumbles, grammar-based cloze test & error detection, vocabulary, etc. Important tips for NMAT Language Skills preparation are:

·         Read novels, journals, newspapers, magazines, educational books, or online articles on any topic to improve your reading speed, vocabulary, and comprehension skills.

·         Be familiar with the different tones of the RC passages such as argumentative, didactic, narrative, cynical, technical, informative, sarcastic, satirical, etc.

·         Learning and practicing basic grammar concepts is essential for scoring high in this section.

·         Practice 10-12 questions every day on para jumbles, close test, and error detection to build confidence in solving such questions.

NMAT 2022 Quantitative Skills Preparation

NMAT Quantitative Skills section comprises 36 questions- around 16 MCQs from DI and 20 MCQs from Arithmetic (P&L, Work & Time, speed, distance, ratio & percentage), Geometry, Algebra, Numbers, and other topics of Quant. Important tips for NMAT Quantitative Skills preparation are:

·         Start with the basics and continue with topics that will help you throughout your preparation for Quant and DI. These include tables, ratio-percentage conversion, simplification, squares & square roots, cubes and cube roots, divisibility rules, remainder theorem, quadratic equations, permutations, combinations, and probability.

·         Try and solve at least 20-30 questions every day for speed and accuracy.

·         Attempt sectional mock tests and sample papers in the time limit of 52 minutes and review it.

·         Learn short tricks and formulas.

 

 NMAT 2022 Logical Reasoning Preparation

NMAT Logical Reasoning section has 36 questions of moderate difficulty. The questions are from various topics of Verbal and Non-verbal Reasoning. Important tips for NMAT Logical Reasoning preparation are:

·         This section required practicing more and more practice. Begin by solving the previous year's question papers.

·         Solve at least 15-20 questions every day on syllogisms, blood relations, seating arrangement, and puzzles.

·         Refer to books by Arun Sharma and join online test series offered by online education portals.

FAQs NMAT Exam 2023

 

Q: How many times is NMAT conducted?

Ans: NMAT is conducted once a year. NMAT 2022 will be held from October 10 to December 19, i.e. a 70-day test window.

 

Q: Who is eligible to take the second phase of NMAT 2022?

Ans: NMAT 2022 is being conducted in one phase only. NMAT 2021 was conducted in two phases due to Covid-19 guidelines. If any guidelines issued for second phase of NMAT, more information will be updated here for your reference.

 

Q: How many times can one register for NMAT?

Ans: Registration for the NMAT exam is accepted only one time in one exam session. However, candidates can have total of three attempts, for which they have to reschedule the test.

 

Q: Is there a form correction facility available for the NMAT application process?

Ans: There is no provision of form correction in the NMAT exam; therefore, all the particulars must be cross-checked before submitting the application form. The 'School Preference' and 'Communication Preference' fields in the application form are enabled after the submission of the application fee.

 

Q: What are all the sections available in the NMAT 2022 syllabus?

Ans: There are three sections available in the NMAT 2022 exam syllabus, namely:

1.       Language Skills

2.       Quantitative Skills

3.       Logical Reasoning

 

Q: Is there any sectional time limit in NMAT exam?

Ans: Yes, the sectional time limit as per the revised NMAT exam 2022 exam pattern out of total 120 minutes is:

·         Language skills- 28 minutes

·         Quantitative Skills- 52 minutes

·         Logical Reasoning- 40 minutes

 

Q: What is the NMAT exam marking scheme?

Ans: In NMAT exam, for every correct answer, the candidate will be awarded one mark and there is no negative marking.

 

Q: How can applicants know about the time slot and date of NMAT 2022?

Ans: The date, time slot and exam venue will be printed on the NMAT admit card. A candidate will have to schedule the test using the 'schedule test' option which is activated upon the receipt of application fee by NMAT. Candidates can then choose the date and time slot of the test as per their convenience for NMAT by GMAC exam. However, this facility is available on a first-come-first-serve basis.

 

Q: What is the procedure to reschedule the NMAT exam?

Ans: Reschedule of the NMAT exam is allowed 72 hours before the scheduled time slot; anytime between 1 August, 2022 to 16 December, 2022. Rescheduling has additional charges and is available on first-cum-first serve basis. The additional fee has to be remitted via credit card only. A confirmation email regarding the approval of the rescheduled test slot will be sent to the candidate's registered email ID.

 

Q: What is the procedure to retake NMAT?

Ans: The option to retake NMAT test is present in the 'Retake' tab in the dashboard of the candidate’ account which is available after the first attempt. Candidates have to wait 15 days before retaking the NMAT exam and have to pay additional application fee for this. Candidates can retake the NMAT exam twice during the 70-day test window.

 

Q: Is there any option to review answers in the NMAT exam?

Ans: Yes, A candidate can review as well as make changes in the answers in different sections only within the slated sectional time limit. There is no option to move back and forth between sections. If the candidate moves to the next section, they will not be able to make any changes in the previous section.

 

Q: What do scaled and percentile scores refer to in the NMAT exam?

Ans: The basic difference between scaled scores and percentile scores in NMAT:

Scaled scores: The test forms are equated to account for any difference in the overall difficulty and the raw test scores are normalized on a common scale to arrive at scaled scores.

Percentile scores: The percentile rank or score is an indicator of the candidate’s score/rank as compared to the score/rank of all the other test-takers.

 

Q: What is the meaning of retake of the NMAT exam?

 

Ans: The process of reapplying for the NMAT exam is known as exam retake. Any NMAT applicant can attempt the test thrice including the first attempt. However, candidates can reappear for the exam, not before 15 days from the first attempt.

 

Q: What is the scheduling and rescheduling of the NMAT exam?

A: Scheduling the NMAT exam refers to choosing a time slot for the test. If the candidate, due to some reason is not able to appear for the test on the allotted date, then there is another provision available in NMAT, known as re-take scheduling / rescheduling. Candidates can also re-take the exam if they wish to by applying for the re-take exam schedule option.

 

Q: Is the NMAT exam tough?

Ans: In comparison with any other MBA entrance exams, the level of difficulty of the NMAT exam is moderate.

 

Q: What is the difference between the NMAT exam and other MBA entrance exams?

A: Unlike other MBA entrance exams, the NMAT exam is held over a period of approximately 90 days and an examinee can appear for the test three times including the first attempt in the same year to improve their score.

 

Q. Are there sectional cut offs for NMIMS admission through NMAT?

Ans. Yes, NMIMS (Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies) releases sectional NMAT cutoffs. Candidates have to mandatorily secure the minimum marks in each section along with the overall cutoff. If one is unable to meet the sectional NMAT cut offs, one is ineligible for admission to MBA courses offered by NMIMS Mumbai and its other campuses.
