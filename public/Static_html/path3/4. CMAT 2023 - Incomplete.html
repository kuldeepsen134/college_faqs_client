About CMAT Examination

 

Common Management Admission Test (CMAT), is a government organized computer based national level MBA / PGDM entrance examination for entry into 1000+ AICTE approved management colleges all over India. Till 2018, CMAT was conducted by AICTE, now the Test is conducted by the National Testing Authority (NTA) constituted by Government of India. Around 60,000 to 70,000 candidates apply for CMAT every year.

CMAT score is accepted by some of the top B-schools in India including Great Lakes Institute of Management, JBIMS, SIMSREE, K J Somaiya Mumbai, Welingkar Mumbai. The CMAT question paper consists of 100 questions from Quantitative Techniques & Data Interpretation, Logical Reasoning, Language Comprehension, General Awareness and Innovation and Entrepreneurship. The total marks of CMAT is 400. CMAT is conducted in Computer Based Test (CBT) mode and the duration of the exam is three hours.

CMAT 2023 Eligibility Criteria

Before applying for CMAT, candidates must ensure that they meet the following eligibility criteria:

·         Candidate must be an Indian resident.

·         Candidate must hold a bachelor's degree of minimum three-year duration or equivalent in any stream from government approved institute/university.

·         Candidates in the final year of their bachelor's degree are also eligible to apply for CMAT exam provided their result will be declared before admission for academic year begins.

·         There is no minimum score criteria for CMAT.

·         There is no upper age limit for applying and appearing in CMAT exam.

CMAT Exam Syllabus

CMAT Syllabus is divided into five sections – Language Comprehension; Quantitative Techniques and Data Interpretation; Logical Reasoning; General Awareness and Innovation & Entrepreneurship. Each of the five sections in CMAT syllabus has equal weightage of 20% with 20 questions each. The candidates must analyze the previous years' question papers carefully, and understand which topics are important. After researching, they should start preparing for the exams.

Syllabus

Important Topics

Language Comprehension

Language usage and their rules, Para jumble, Syntax and correct grammar, Vocabulary, Fill in blanks, Idioms, Reading comprehension, Anonyms, and Synonyms.

Data interpretation, Problem-Solving, Data Sufficiency, Arithmetic, Geometry, Modern Mathematics, Algebra, and Number Systems

Logical Reasoning

Family tree, Arrangement, Numeric Grid, Coding, and Series

General Awareness

Personalities, Books and Authors, History, Politics, Economics, Business, Sports, Social Developments, Entertainment, Current Affairs of National and International Economy, Personalities in News

Innovation & Entrepreneurship

Innovative business practices, Government plans and policies, global entrepreneurs and personalities etc.

 

CMAT 2023 Exam Preparation Tips

Candidates planning to appear for CMAT exam 2023 must start there preparation six to nine months before the entrance exam. They must devise an exam preparation plan which should include:

1.       Covering CMAT syllabus - CMAT syllabus is similar to other MBA entrance exams. It includes Verbal Ability, Reading Comprehension, Data Interpretation, Logical Reasoning, Quantitative Aptitude and General Knowledge. Apart from these subjects, there is an Innovation and Entrepreneurship which was earlier optional is now mandatory like other sections. Candidates must start CMAT preparation by picking the tougher topics first. Once they are thorough with the tough topics they should move to easier topics.

 

2.       Practicing mock tests and sample papers – After completing the CMAT syllabus, make sure to practice CMAT mock tests to analyse your preparation level. Practice 25-30 mock tests to improve your speed and accuracy in solving questions.

 

3.       Revision of important topics – Along with mock test, revision should also continue for all topics. Keep aside an hour every day for revision of topics. In the last 15-20 days before CMAT exam, dedicate maximum time on revision and mock tests so that everything is fresh.

CMAT 2023 Answer Key

CMAT answer key will be released by the NTA on the exam website within one week of the exam. The answer key comprises of the CMAT question paper and individual response sheet. Students who has given CMAT exam would be able to download the CMAT 2023 answer key from the NTA website by using their login credentials. The process to download CMAT answer key 2023 is:

·         Visit the CMAT-NTA website - https://cmat.nta.nic.in

·         Click on ‘CMAT 2023 Answer Key Download’

·         Enter application number and date of birth or password

·         Click on ‘Answer Key’ link reflecting on the dashboard

·         Download the CMAT 2023 answer key

After the release of ‘Answer Key’ student have the opportunity to challenge to official answer key. Under this provision, any candidate can challenge the official answer key by:

·         Filling an online form on NTA website

·         Furnishing document to substantiate their claim

·         Pay the requisite fees

If the claim is found to be valid, the CMAT answer key will be revised. CMAT result and final answer key will be prepared after analysing the objections received.

 

CMAT 2023 Result

CMAT result 2023 will be declared online by the NTA on the exam website within two to three days of Answer Key. Along with result, the NTA will also release the merit list and all India ranks. Given below are the steps to download CMAT scorecard cum result:

·         Log on to CMAT official website

·         Click on 'CMAT-2023 has been Declared' tab

·         Enter your CMAT 2023 login credentials (application number and password or date of birth)

·         Click on 'Submit' and download the CMAT scorecard

FAQs CMAT Exam 2023

 

Q: What is CMAT?

Ans: CMAT is one of the important national level MBA entrance exams for admission to MBA / PGDM in 1000+ AICTE approved management institutes. CMAT 2023 will be conducted by National Testing Agency (NTA) as computer based test of three hours duration.

 

Q. When is CMAT 2023?

Ans: CMAT is held once a year and CMAT 2023 is expected to be held in the month of March or April 2023. The exam is conducted at various exam centres across 124 cities in India. You can choose the test city according to your preference.

 

Q: Is CMAT conducted online or offline?

Ans: CMAT exam is a computer based test mode conducted online at designated examination centres only. Candidates are required to punch in their answers with the help of keypad and mouse of the computer system provided.

 

Q: What are all the sections available in the CMAT 2023 syllabus?

Ans: There are five sections available in the CMAT exam syllabus, namely:

1.       Quantitative Techniques & Data Interpretation

2.       Logical Reasoning

3.       Language Comprehension & Verbal Ability

4.       General Awareness

5.       Innovation and Entrepreneurship

 

Q: Can I go back and forth between the sections during CMAT exam?

Ans: Yes, you can because there will be no sectional wise time limit.

 

 Q: When is the last date to apply for CMAT 2023?

Ans: The CMAT registration 2023 schedule is not yet announced. NTA conducts CMAT exam and schedule of CMAT 2023 will be announced in January or February 2023. The application form filling window will be live for about one month for candidates to register and pay the fees for the exam.

 

Q: What are the eligibility criteria to apply for CMAT?

Ans: Any candidate who has secured passing marks in graduation can apply for CMAT. Candidates must ensure that they have cleared graduation. Candidates in the final year of their bachelor's degree are also eligible to apply for CMAT exam provided their result will be declared before admission for academic year begins.

 

Q: Is there any age restriction for appearing for CMAT exam?

Ans: No, there are no age restrictions for the CMAT exam 2023.

 

Q: For how long is CMAT score valid for admission?

Ans: Similar to other MBA entrance exams, CMAT score is also valid for one year.

 

Q. How many colleges accept CMAT score for admission?

Ans: Over 1,000+ MBA / PGDM colleges across India accept CMAT score for admission. These includes some of the top colleges like JBIMS - Mumbai, Goa Institute of Management - Goa, KJ Somaiya Institute of Management - Mumbai, SIMSREE - Mumbai, Great Lakes Institute of Management - Chennai, PUMBA - Pune, etc.

 

Q: What are the reservation criteria for CMAT?

Ans: There is no reservation criterion for CMAT exam. However, CMAT participating institutes have to abide by reservation criteria for educational institutions set by the Government of India during admission process.

 

Q: What is the CMAT application fee?

 

Ans: CMAT application fee is Rs. 2,000 for general category male student. For general category female/reserved category students the application fee is Rs. 1,000.

 

Q: Is there a provision of rechecking or revaluating CMAT answer sheets?

Ans: No, there is no provision of rechecking or revaluating CMAT answer sheets. Marks allotted to candidates in the scorecard will be final. However, once the Answer Key is released, a student has the chance to raise a claim by following the due process and if claim is found to be correct than it is considered.

 

Q: Till when will CMAT scorecard is available for download on the NTA website?

Ans: The CMAT result is available for 60 days from the result date on the CMAT-NTA website. After two months, they will have to put special requisition with the exam authorities for CMAT scorecard.

 

Q: Will the NTA release CMAT 2023 merit list after result declaration?

Ans: Yes, the NTA is expected to release CMAT 2023 merit list along with the result. The merit list will be released in the form of PDF, containing each test taker's section-wise scores, overall score and overall percentile.

 

 

Q: Is there any difference between CMAT score and percentile?

Ans: Starting from CMAT 2022, NTA has stopped giving percentile. Students are only issued with score and rank.

 

Q: How many times can I attempt CMAT?

Ans: Since CMAT is an annual exam, you can attempt it only once in the particular year. However, there is no limit on number of times you can attempt for CMAT.

 

Q: How many candidates appear for CMAT?

Ans: Around 50,000 to 60,000 student appear for CMAT every year.

 

Q: Is there will be negative marking in the CMAT 2023 exam?

Ans: Yes, one mark will get reduced for each incorrect answer. For every correct answer a student will get 4 marks. In total the CMAT exam is of 400 marks with 100 questions equally divided into five sections.

 

Q: How can I request to change the exam centre?

Ans: Once CMAT admit card is issued, no request for change in exam centre will be entertained.

 

Q: Can I carry a watch to the CMAT exam centre?

Ans: No, candidates are not allowed to carry few items such as a wristwatch, mobile, calculator, etc to the CMAT test centre.

 

Q. Can I change preferred test centre after submitting a form?

Ans: No, once the application form has been submitted you cannot change the test centre. However, test centre is allotted as per the preference given by the student. However, if NTA announces application correction date, then you may be allowed to change your CMAT test centre.

 

Q. Do government MBA colleges in India accept CMAT score?

Ans: Yes, many government colleges like PUMBA - Pune, Indian Institute of Information Technology - Allahabad, NIT - Rourkela etc.
