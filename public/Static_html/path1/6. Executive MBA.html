Executive MBA

‘Executive MBA’ or ‘EMBA’ is generally a premium program offered to senior executives with extensive work experience. Professionals who don’t wish to leave their well-paying full-time jobs opt for this programme for earning MBA credentials.

The course program structure is designed in such a way that a senior working executive spends 50-60 days on campus attending classes and completes the rest of the EMBA program while working. There is not much difference in the course content of the regular MBA/PGDM programme and the Executive MBA/PGDM programme. However, both these courses are designed for different people with different needs.

Executive MBA / PGDM: Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility criteria for Executive MBA / PGDM are similar to that of Regular MBA /PGDM apart from the work experience factor. Work experience carries a lot of weightage during the selection process. Some of the basic criteria for Executive MBA / PGDM are below:

·         Any candidate who has completed bachelor’s degree with  minimum 50 per cent aggregate (45% for reserved categories) or equivalent from a recognised university. In many institutes, there is no minimum cut off marks for Executive MBA admission.

·         The candidate must have at least two years of work experience at managerial or executive level. Work experience in part-time mode is not considered for this.

·         Number of years of work experience varies from two to five years for every institute. The candidate must check the eligibility criteria of the preferred B-schools before applying.

 

 

 

Skills Required for Executive MBA / PGDM

Executive MBA / PGDM candidates not only need to prove they are qualified managers but also that they have proper knowledge of their fields. Some of the most important skills required for Executive MBA / PGDM programme are:

Leadership: Professionals working as a manager are required to possess strong leadership skills as they need to manage a team. Being a good leader is as important as being good in their field.

Patience: Executive MBA / PGDM candidates need to learn to be patient about their work, assignments and profession but also need to abide by the timelines by keeping patience.

Dedication: As a manager, responsibilities can only be fulfilled if one is completely committed to his/her work. There are certain times when the problems are complicated and one has to handle a team also, at such moments u need to be dedicated towards your end goal.

Teamwork: Teamwork is yet another important skill that a senior professional should possess since corporate projects are not about working alone but with a team.

Communication: Both verbal and written communication is an integral part of a manager’s activity. While leading a team of professionals with different mindsets and backgrounds, it is important for a manager to be clear and concise about his/her instructions.

Execuitve MBA FAQs

 

Q: What is the difference between an MBA and executive MBA?

Ans: MBA is a master degree course offered by the University, to develop understanding of basics of business and management. An executive MBA is a post graduate program for the working people.

 

Q: Who is eligible for executive MBA?

Ans: Candidates with work experience of 2 – 5 years at a managerial or executive level  is the most important criteria. Apart from this it is necessary to a bachelor's degree with at least 50% aggregate or equivalent from a recognized university are eligible for executive MBA / PGDM.

 

Q: Is an executive MBA worth the money?

Ans: The scope for executive MBA / PGDM is decent as it provides a gamut of opportunities to grow. Executive MBA holders are best suited for senior-level managerial and consulting jobs and entrepreneurship. Candidates are hired by all kind of companies and business houses for profiles in senior management in domains like Finance, Marketing, Sales, Human Resources, IT, Digital Marketing, Business Analysis, Operations, Banking, Insurance, Consulting, etc.

 

Q: What is the highest salary of an executive MBA?

Ans: The salary for executive MBA / PGDM candidates ranges from Rs. 12 lakh per annum to Rs. 40 lakh per annum.

 

Q: Is EMBA equivalent to a Master's degree?

Ans: Yes, executive MBA is equivalent to a master’s degree.

 

Q: Is an Executive MBA from IIM worth doing?

Ans: Completing an executive MBA from IIM can cost a lot but it is totally worthy, as the ROI is also quite impressive.

 

Q: Is a CAT score required for the executive MBA?

Ans: If you are targeting IIMs for executive MBA program, it is mandatory to appear for CAT and also clear the cut-off. For any other college / university, a student needs to check about the eligibility criteria of that college / university.

 

Q: When can I do an Executive MBA?

Ans: Working professionals with at least 2-5 years of experience can apply for executive MBA courses can do executive MBA /  PGDM at anytime in there career.

 

Q: Which companies hire executive MBAs?

Ans: There are countless companies that offer jobs to executive MBAs. Some of the top recruiters are: HCL, Infosys, Vedanta, Trident Group, Grey Orange, KPMG, Reliance Industries, Larsen & Toubro (L&T), Cadbury, UltraTech Cement, Mahindra and Mahindra, ITC, Tata Motor, Godrej & Boyce Mfg. Co. etc.
