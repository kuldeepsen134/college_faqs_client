Distance MBA

Distance MBA course is a flexible and affordable program which does not require you to attend regular daily classes. It is one of the most preferred alternatives for MBA aspirants who can’t attend regular classes. The classes take place in a hybrid method. Students may be asked to visit the institute or centers during weekends.

Nowadays most of the universities offer distance MBA courses and have established placements cells to take care of a candidate’s better placement. Some of the popular distance MBA Colleges in India include IGNOU, Annamalai University, NMIMS Global Access School for Continuing Education, Symbiosis Centre for Distance Learning, IMT-CDL Ghaziabad, SikkimSCDL, etc.

Why choose Distance MBA?

One of the main advantages of pursuing an MBA through correspondence is that it allows the students to manage their professional lives and careers simultaneously. Students can easily manage to complete their studies without any constraints. Through distance MBA, the students can study from anywhere. This helps them save money and time. The time saved can be used to prepare for the exams or to improve their skills.

The distance MBA programme is cheaper than the regular MBA programs as it allows the students to pursue their studies without having to think about the fees. The study materials are organized in a way that allows the students to easily find the current and ongoing topics. One of the main advantages of a distance MBA is that the students do not have to quit their job to get an MBA. They can study and earn money at the same time. After finishing their MBA, they can find another job

 

 

Distance MBA Degree Eligibility Criteria

For admission to the distance MBA programme, the candidates should have a good score on the various entrance tests or qualify through institute level entrance exam. Many colleges offer direct admission basis academics merit and work experience.

The common eligibility criteria for the distance MBA degree programme is that the candidates should be holding a bachelor’s degree in any discipline with 50% marks and 45% marks in case of reserved category.

IGNOU: The candidates must be a graduate and should have scored at least 50 per cent marks in their graduation (45 per cent marks for reserved category candidates). Candidates should have passed the OPENMAT examination (which is conducted by the National Testing Agency). There is no age limit for admission to the MBA programme offered by IGNOU

Distance MBA FAQs

 

Q: What is the required educational qualification to enroll in a distance MBA?

Answer: Candidates who have successfully completed graduation with minimum 50 per cent in any stream (45 per cent marks for reserved category students)  are eligible for distance MBA programme.

 

Q: What is the average fee of a distance MBA?

Ans: Fees is always dependent upon college / institute. The average fee of the distance MBA ranges from Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 200,000.

 

Q: What is the average salary offered to graduates of distance MBA?

Ans: The average salary offered to freshly graduates of distance MBA is Rs. 200,000 to Rs. 5,00,000 per annum, however candidates with work experience do get extra advantage with salary upto Rs. 10,00,000 per annum.
