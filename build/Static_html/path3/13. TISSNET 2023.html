About TISSNET Examination

 

TISSNET is the mandatory entrance test for M.A. Admission in Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS). It is a university level entrance exam conducted for admission to MA programmes in the School of Management and Labour Studies. The MA programme in Human Resources Management and Labour Relations offered at TISS campuses is at par with the management programmes offered by top MBA colleges in India. The entrance exam is held in CBT mode and the exam paper comprises questions from English Proficiency, Mathematical Skills & Logical Reasoning, and General Awareness.

Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS) is conducting TISS National Entrance Test (TISS-NET) for admission to 53 Masters’ Degree (M.A.) Programs offered at its Mumbai, Tuljapur, Guwahati and Hyderabad campuses for the 2023-2025 batch. TISS has 21 schools and 45 centres offering variety of programmes.

TISSNET 2023 Eligibility Criteria

The eligibility criteria for candidates appearing in TISSNET 2023 exam has been given below:

·         The candidate must possess a bachelor's degree or equivalent from a recognised university

·         Candidates, who will complete their final year of bachelor's degree/equivalent by June 2023, can also apply. Those who are in the final year of the degree programme are required to produce the mark sheets of all previous semesters

·         There is no defined age limit for appearing in TISSNET 2023 exam.

·         Candidates applying for Master of Arts in (Organisation Development, Change and Leadership) should have at least 2-year work experience in any field (after graduation).

 

TISSNET 2023 Application Process

TISSNET application form is available in both Online and Offline mode. The details of both the process is given below.

TISSNET 2023 Online Registration

1.       Visit the official website for TISS admissions - https://admissions.tiss.edu/

2.       Register an account on TISS portal by providing your email ID and a password will be sent to you from TISS on the same ID.

3.       Login with the above-mentioned credentials in order to fill the form.

4.       Enter your personal and academic details in the online form, choose your preferred courses and exam cities.

5.       Next, upload your signature and passport size photograph as per the defined specifications. Check TISSNET Photo Size and Other Details.

6.       Also, upload all the documents required as per your Reservation Category.

7.       After that, pay the application fee as per the suitable mode.

8.       When you are done, click ‘Submit’.

9.       Post successful registration, you will receive a confirmation mail on your registered ID.

TISSNET 2023 Offline Registration

Special provisions have been made for candidates having limited access to the internet (North-East, Jammu & Kashmir, Jharkhand, etc). Candidates from remote areas can also avail this facility. Such candidates can fill offline form. The steps to follow for TISSNET Registration 2023:

1.       Send request for the form through speed post along with the application fee to TISS Mumbai Campus at To Assistant Registrar (Academic), Tata Institute of Social Sciences, Deonar Mumbai, 400088

2.       Within a week, the application form will be sent to you via speed post.

3.       Fill TISSNET Application Form 2022 and attach xeroxed copies of all the required documents. Make sure that they are attested by any School Headmaster or a Principal of College or a Gazetted Officer.

4.       After that, send the application form via speed post at the same address at which you requested for the form.

5.       Information related to TISSNET Admit Card and other official communication will be sent to the registered Email address.

Candidates appearing for TISSNET must keep in mind certain instructions related to the exam day and test centre given below:

·         Reach atleast an hour before at the test centre.

·         Do maintain COVID 19 safety guidelines.

·         Do not carry prohibited items like books, cellphones or any electronic gadgets, Bluetooth devices or calculators are not allowed in the exam centre.

TISSNET 2023 Cutoff

TISSNET Cutoffs for MA (HRM) and MA (ODCL) is expected to stay in the 69-72 range marks. Though TISS does not have sectional cut off, in any section except GK, where at least 35% (14 marks out of 40 marks) is mandatory.

·         General Awareness: 25-27 marks

·         English Proficiency: 27-28 marks

·         Maths and Logical Reasoning: 23-25 marks

·         Total: 70-75 marks

The cut-off score varies from subject to subject. It also depends on the number of seats available in each course in each campus. The number of candidates appearing for the exam each year is also a factor.

TISSNET 2022 Counselling Process

The national written entrance exam is just the first round for selection in TISS. Candidates also need to appear for Pre Interview Test (TISSMAT/TISSPAT) and Personal Interview (PI) rounds to get finally qualified. These admission rounds of TISSNET will be held separately for each campus of TISS. It is only after all these rounds are completed that the TISS authorities will come up with the final selection list. The final score will be based on performance in all the rounds.

Applicants will also need to produce all required documents at the time of admission. After your documents are verified, the TISS authorities will require a candidate to clear a medical check-up before admission is confirmed.

FAQs – TISSNET Exam 2023

 

Q. When will TISSNET 2023 registration commence?

Ans. The TISSNET 2023 registration will commence tentatively in third week of November 2022.

 

Q. What is TISSNET 2023 registration last date?

Ans. The last date for TISSNET 2023 registration is expected to be in second week of January 2023.

 

Q: What are the sections of TISSNET 2022 examination?

Ans: There are three sections for TISSNET exam - General awareness, English Proficiency and Mathematics and Logical Reasoning.

 

Q. When will be the TISSNET 2023 exam conducted?

Ans. TISSNET 2023 exam is expected to be held on January 21, 2023 in CBT mode.

 

Q. How many people appear for the TISSNET exam?

Ans: Every year nearly 40,000 candidates appear for the TISSNET exam. Among them, around 17,000 register for the MA (HRM & LR).

 

Q: In case, I need to change the test centre later, what is the procedure.

Ans: Only genuine cases are considered for examination centre change. For the same, candidates should reach out to the examination authority or write to them at pgadmission@tiss.edu.

 

Q: What is a good score in TISSNET?

Ans. A score of about 70 is considered a good score in TISSNET Exam.

 

Q: Is there any management quota available at TISS?

Ans: No, TISS does not provide admission based on any sort of management quota.

 

Q: How many courses are offered through TISSNET?

Ans. There are more than 55 courses that you can apply to through TISSNET Exam.

 

Q. Can I apply for multiple courses in TISSNET?

Ans: Yes, candidates are allowed to apply for multiple courses in the TISSNET. They need to mention them in the application form and then pay for all of those courses during the process of registration itself.

 

Q. What is the total number of seats in the MA HRM and LR course?

Ans: There are 60 seats allotted for the MA HRM and LR course in TISS.

 

Q. Which are all the other programs offered by the TISS?

Ans: TISS offer multiple fulltime programs which are M.A./ MHA / M.Sc./ Law program. In January month, M.Phil. /Ph.D. will be offered in June Certificate / Diploma.

 

Q: Can international students apply for TISSNET?

Ans. Yes, international students can apply for TISSNET exam but their admission process is different from that of Indian students.

 

Q: When will the final list of shortlisted candidates be announced?

Ans: The merit list is likely to be announced in the third week of June 2023.

 

Q: Can I directly appear for the second round of TISSNET if I have cleared CAT exam?

Ans. No, even CAT qualified candidates are not given directly entry to the second round. One has to clear the first round of TISSNET 2022 written exam to be eligible for the second round.

 

Q: What is TISSMAT?

Ans: TISSMAT is a pre-Interview test of 45 minutes duration carrying around 36 MCQs from the areas of Comprehension, Management Data Interpretation, Business Awareness and Descriptive Writing.  Candidates who qualify TISSNET appear for TISSMAT.

 

Q: Is there any negative marking in TISSNET examination?

Ans: No, there is no negative marking in TISSNET. However, 0.25 marks is deducted for every incorrect answer in the TISSMAT.

 

Q. Where will the PIT/ PI rounds be held?

Ans: The PIT and the PI rounds for the admission into the TISS will be held at three locations. They are Mumbai, Hyderabad, and Guwahati.

 

Q: How is the score aggregated for final selection?

Ans: The scores for TISSNET, online assessment and OPI will be aggregated in the ratio of 50:20:30 for final selection.

 

Q: What kinds of questions are asked in Personal Interview rounds?

Ans: TISS conducts PI round to evaluate candidates’ interpersonal skills, understanding of the programme and examine past academic records.

 

Q. How can I check my TISSNET result?

Ans. TISSNET 2023 result will be declared soon after the exam is conducted. Candidates can download the scorecard by entering the login credentials on TISS official website.

 

Q. Does TISS released sectional cutoff?

Ans. TISS released sectional cutoff for General Awareness section only. For the other sections candidates must score the minimum marks prescribed. 

 

Q. Does TISS offer admission to MBA programme through TISSNET?

Ans. TISS offers MA in HRM & LR which is equivalent to MBA programme offered by other management institutes.
