About IIFT Examination

 

Indian Institute of Foreign Trade Admission Test-2023 popularly known as IIFT Exam 2023, is the MBA Entrance Exam for Admission to its flagship MBA – International Business (IB) programme which will have a total intake of more than 570 seats in 2023 in its three campuses namely Delhi, Kolkata and Kakinada. MBA (IB) is presently offered at IIFT Delhi and Kolkata campuses with a total approved 500+ seats. 

Every year around 60,000 students from all disciplines including Engineering, Commerce, Humanities, Law and others take the IIFT admission test to get MBA admission in Indian Institute of Foreign Trade. The IIFT paper consists of questions from Verbal Ability & Reading Comprehension, Quantitative Ability, Data Interpretation & Logical Reasoning and General Awareness. Duration of this online exam is two hours. IIFT accepts foreign/NRI/PIO candidates for admission on the basis of GMAT score.

IIFT 2023 Eligibility Criteria

IIFT Exam eligibility criteria are as follows:

·         Recognized Bachelor’s degree of minimum 3 years’ duration in any discipline with minimum 50% marks [45% in case of the candidates belonging to Scheduled Caste (SC), Scheduled Tribe (ST) and Persons with Disability (PwD) categories] (worked out on the basis of aggregate marks obtained in all the papers).

·         Candidates appearing for a qualifying examination can also apply subject to submission of proof of requisite qualification by 7th October 2022.

·         There is no age limit for appearing in this examination.

IIFT 2023 Exam Preparation Tips

To prepare for IIFT 2023 exam, one needs to make a plan in which, ample time is allotted to all the 4 topics of the syllabus.

·         Start with studying fundamental concepts of important topics of every section.

·         Once the IIFT syllabus is covered, move to solving practice tests and sample papers.

·         For Quantitative section, one needs to be thorough with the concepts and formulae as well as all types of questions.

·         For Reasoning, Data Interpretation and Verbal Ability, one needs to practice sample questions from the beginning to the end of the IIFT exam preparation phase.

·         General Awareness section can be picked up in the last leg of IIFT exam preparation as it is memory based and easy to handle.

·         Attempt mock tests and analyze learning pattern as per the score.

IIFT 2023 Selection Procedure

Candidates who clear the cut-off are invited for the selection process which consists of essay writing, group discussion and interview:

Essay Writing -

·         Candidates need to write an essay based on some topic of General or business affairs.

·         Candidates’ are tested for writing skills and conceptual knowledge about the topics.

·         The average duration of this round is 20 minutes

·         Essay length is usually 300 words

GD (Group Discussion) -

·         Qualified candidates from Essay Writing are divided into groups of 10-12 participants.

·         Candidates are given a topic related to current or business affairs.

·         Candidates’ are tested on ability to take initiative, ability to effectively communicate etc.

PI (Personal Interview) -

·         The Personal Interview round is one on one round

·         Panelists adjudge the candidates on the basis of their persona and ability to stay focused under pressure

·         Duration of the PI round is 10-15 minutes

FAQs – IIFT Exam 2022

 

Q: When will the IIFT 2023 exam be conducted?

Ans: The IIFT 2023 exam is expected to be conducted on 11th December, 2022. The NTA will announce IIFT exam schedule in the first week of September 2022 and registration process may begum by second week of September, 2022.

 

Q. What is the duration of IIFT 2023?

Ans: IIFT 2023 exam duration is 120 minutes for attempting 110 questions divided into 4 sections- VARC, QA, DILR, and GK.

 

Q: What is the IIFT selection criterion?

Ans: The selection criteria of IIFT include IIFT exam score and selection process rounds which are Essay Writing, GD and PI. Candidates who clear the entrance test are shortlisted for Essay Writing, GD and PI. The final selection is done on the basis of candidate's overall performance in all selection rounds as per the weightage of each round. .

 

Q. Is there any sectional cut off for the test?

Ans: Yes, candidates must clear sectional as well as overall cut off of the written test in order to qualify for further admission process of IIFT. The expected sectional cut off over the years is about: English Comprehension 8.75, General Knowledge & Awareness 8.02, Logical reasoning 8.68, Quantitative Aptitude 4.34. This is just a tentative score and final score will be decided by IIFT.

 

Q: Is IIFT score valid for admission to other institutes?

Ans: No, IIFT is an institute level entrance exam conducted by NTA. The candidates who appear for IIFT entrance exam, can seek admission to MBA (IB) programme at IIFT Delhi, IIFT Kolkata and IIFT Kakinada only.

 

Q. Is the reservation system followed by the university?

Ans: Yes, the university admits candidates based on the reservation system as stated by the government of India.

 

Q. Is there a management quota for the MBA[IB] program?

Ans: No, there is no management quota for the MBA[IB] program at IIFT.

 

Q: What is the difficulty level of IIFT exam?

Ans: The IIFT exam question paper is of moderate to high difficulty level. The Quantitative Ability and General Awareness sections are usually tough. Candidate also needs to keep in mind the scoring pattern of each section.

 

Q: I am a final year graduation student; can I apply for IIFT exam?

Ans: Yes, graduation final year students are also eligible to apply for the IIFT exam. However, your admission will be provisional until you submit your Graduation certificate before the last date of document submission fixed by IIFT.

 

Q: Is there any age-limit to appear in IIFT exam?

Ans: No, there is no age limit to appear in the IIFT entrance exam. The only eligibility criteria for IIFT exam is Graduation with minimum of 50 per cent in aggregate. Candidates having work experience can also appear for the exam.

 

Q. Is the change in the center of the examination permitted?

Ans: Candidates can apply for a change in center online only. They must pay the fees of INR 1000 for the same. No requests will be considered offline.

 

Q: In which cities IIFT exam 2023 will be conducted?

Ans: The IIFT exam 2023 will be conducted in 68 cities including Ahmedabad, Allahabad, Bangalore, Bhubaneshwar, Chandigarh, Chennai, Coimbatore, Delhi, Dehradun, Guwahati, Hyderabad, Indore, Jaipur, Jamshedpur, Kochi, Kolkata, Lucknow, Mumbai, Nagpur and Visakhapatnam among others.

 

Q. Will the centers for the Interview be the same as that of the Written test?

Ans: No, the centers for interviews will not be the same as those of written test. The selection process round will be conducted in Ahmedabad, Bengaluru, Chennai, Delhi, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Lucknow and Mumbai.

 

Q: Is the syllabus for IIFT similar to CAT?

Ans: Yes, the IIFT syllabus is similar to CAT syllabus for English, Logical Reasoning and Quantitative Aptitude. However, IIFT aspirants have to study General Awareness too as it is one of the four sections of IIFT question paper which is similar to XAT.

 

Q: How tough is cracking IIFT?

Ans: Cracking IIFT is considered tough as around 60,000 students take the entrance exam while there are about 500 plus seats on offer at all the three campuses of the institute. However, if you prepare for the IIFT exam well, you can crack it.

 

Q: What is the IIFT exam application fee?

Ans: IIFT exam fee for candidates of different categories is different. Check category-wise IIFT application fees below:

·         General/OBC candidates: INR 2,500

·         SC/ST/PH candidates: INR 1,000

·         Foreign National/NRI/Children of NRI candidates: US $200 (INR 15,000)
