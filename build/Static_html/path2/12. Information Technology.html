MBA in Information Technology

MBA in Information Technology has become a preferred choice of specialisation among those students who plan to be a part of the growing internet-based industries that include e-commerce platforms and technology companies. It deals with the study of IT management, computer science and its allied sectors to churn out skilled IT managers and provides knowledge of leadership and a high level of concentration in IT.

MBA in Information Technology enables students to use their knowledge from basic management to specialised core competencies such as finance, sales and marketing, account management, human resource, and other different aspects of IT business. MBA in Information Technology has ample job opportunities in the technology sectors and candidates are hired by MNCs, industries, government departments, and businesses that are reliant on IT.

MBA in Information Technology: Skills Required

New technologies are being introduced daily and it is important for students to keep up with the latest software’s and the changes in IT industry. MBA / PGDM in Information Technology requires students to develop various skills in business management as well as IT. Some of the key skills are:

1.       Awareness of market and products

2.       Computer and digital literacy

3.       Data management and analytics

4.       Strategic mindset

5.       Leadership skills

6.       Data mining & coding

7.       Observation skills

8.       Analytical and Critical thinking skills

FAQs – MBA in Information Technology

 

Q: What is MBA in Information Technology?

Ans: MBA / PGDM in Information Technology is a postgraduate programme that deals with the study of IT management and its related sectors to stir up skilled IT managers by providing knowledge of leadership and a high level of concentration in IT.

 

Q: What is the eligibility criterion for MBA in Information Technology?

Ans: The eligibility criterion for MBA / PGDM in Information Technology is graduation or equivalent from a recognised university. Most of the institutes have minimum score criteria of 50 per cent in graduation (45 per cent for reserved category).

 

Q: Are MBA in Information Technology and MBA in Information Management same course?

Ans: No, Information Technology course imparts knowledge in technical aspects whereas; Information Management includes managerial aspect of the department.

 

Q: Is it necessary to have Science background for MBA in Information Technology?

Ans: No, it is not necessary to have Science background to pursue MBA / PGDM in Information technology. Candidates from any discipline can opt for IT specialization. However, some colleges do give preference to students of BCA, B. Tech, and B. Sc progammes.

 

Q: What's the scope of MBA in Information Technology in India?

Ans: The scope of MBA in Information Technology in India is very bright as the country is fast moving towards digitization. After completing MBA in Information Technology one can get hired at managerial level in IT and Operations domains and with experience can gain multifold opportunities.

 

Q: Can MBA in Information Technology be through correspondence?

Ans: No, most of the MBA colleges offering this specialization have full-time degree programmes. However, there are short-term certification courses which offer basic training in this area.

 

Q: Which are the top colleges in India for MBA in International Business?

Ans: Some of the popular B-schools offering MBA / PGDM in Information technology are Jamnalal Bajaj Institute of Management Studie (JBIMS), Mumbai, KJ Somaiya Institute of Management Studies and Research, Mumbai, Symbiosis Centre for Information Technology, Pune, Management Development Institute, Gurgaon, Institute of Management Technology, Ghaziabad (more names of our choice to be added).

 

Q: What is the admission process of MBA in Information Technology?

Ans: Admission process of MBA / PGDM in any top B-school is similar. Student has to clear the cut-off of entrance exam and this is followed by personality assessment through GD/WAT and PI.

 

Q: What is the average fee of MBA in Information Technology course?

Ans: Fees is highly college dependent and also depends upon the college being a government or a private college. The fee ranges from Rs. 1 Lac for in government colleges to to Rs. 20 lac in private colleges.

 

Q: What is the average salary of an Information Technology Manager in India?

Ans: The salary of an International Marketing Manager could be around Rs. 5 lac per annum, however remuneration increases as one gains work experience.

 

Q: Do colleges offering full time MBA / PGDM in Information Technology provide campus placement?

Ans: IT and ITeS related organization are the major recruiters of MBA in Information technology graduates. Some of the top companies who hore are Google, IBM, Dell, Oracle, HP, Accenture, TCS, HCL , Infotech, Intel, Amazon, etc,

 

Q: Which entrance exam score is required for admission to MBA in Information Technolgy course?

Ans: For admission to MBA in Information Technology courses offered by colleges in India, candidates can appear for CAT, GMAT, XAT, CMAT and MAT.
