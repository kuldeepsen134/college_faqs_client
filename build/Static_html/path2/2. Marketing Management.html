MBA in Marketing

MBA in Sales & Marketing or MBA in Marketing, both are similar. It is one of the oldest specialization of management study and one of the most popular among students. Every business, be it national or international, requires a marketing team that could popularise the brand through activities, including promotions, advertisements, sales, media and entertainment.

With the advent of new technology and social media, online or digital marketing, has become the trend among new-age students as companies use social media world for brand promotions. This has further expanded the scope of MBA in Marketing. Scope for MBA in marketing in India is ever-expanding given that marketing is a vast field.

MBA in Marketing: Skills Required

The top skills required for an MBA / PGDM in Marketing are as follows:

1.  Interpersonal and Leadership Skills - Interpersonal and leadership skills are essential as candidates must be equipped with strong leadership skills. This empowers them and their team to work with full potential, make decisions and strategize.

2.  Strategic Thinking – Market, consumer and demand keep evolving, so to think out of the box and move from a functional role to a "Bigger picture".

3.  Entrepreneurial Skills - This skill will help in spotting a gap in the market, judge whether it is worth filling the creativity and technical understanding for the dynamic growth of the organization.

4.  Communication skills – A good manager must possess excellent written and verbal communication skills, so that he / she is manage the communication across all the levels.

FAQs – MBA in Marketing

 

Q. What is the career scope after MBA / PGDM in Marketing?

Ans: MBA / PGDM in Marketing will open the door for jobs at executive but also managerial positions in good companies. After completion a candidate can get a job as a Marketing Manager, Brand Manager, Product Manager, Digital Marketing Manager, Sales Manager, etc.

 

Q. Who is eligible to pursue MBA / PGDM in Marketing?

Ans: Anyone with a legitimate graduation degree in any discipline with minimum 50 per cent marks is eiligible to pursue MBA / PGDM in Marketing. For reserved category students, the minimum marks is 45 per cent.

 

Q. Are there any job opportunities other than sales after MBA / PGDM in Marketing?

Ans: Marketing as a department is found in all the organization be it any sector, any industry. This opens up a range of job opportunities in industries like retail, tourism, banking, media, hospitality, advertising, etc.

Q. What is the course fee of MBA / PGDM in Marketing?

Ans: MBA / PGDM in Marketing is offered as a specialization in B-schools. The course fee for entire 2 years ranges from Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 25 Lac.

Q. What is the average salary of a fresher after completing MBA / PGDM in Marketing?

Ans: The average salary of MBA / PGDM in Marketing ranges between Rs. 5 Lac to Rs. 20 Lac per annum.

 

Q: What is the admission process for MBA / PGDM in Marketing course?

Ans: Admission process is mostly dependent on the B-school a student is targeting. In general the admission processed comprises of entrance test followed up by group discussion and personal interview.

 

Q: Which are the top colleges for MBA / PGDM in Marketing in India?

Ans: Top colleges in India for MBA / PGDM in Marketing include S P Jain Institute of Management and Research (SPJIMR), Mumbai, Institute of Management Technology (IMT), Ghaziabad, Jamnalal Bajaj Institute of Management Studies (JBIMS), Mumbai, K J Somaiya Institute of Management Studies & Research, Mumbai, (more names of our tie up colleges to be written)

 

Q: What do you study in MBA / PGDM in Marketing?

Ans: Apart from the core subjects students in Marketing specialisation study subjects like Applied Operations Research, International Business Management, Enterprise Research Planning, Supply Chain Management, Logistics Management, Product Design and Development, Total Quality Management, Strategic Management, Project Management, Business Process Management, Materials Management, Maintenance Management and Project Report of Specialization.

 

Q: Which are the top companies for recruitment for MBA / PGDM in Marketing?

Ans: Marketing is a vital department of every company across industries; hence there is no dearth of jobs in the market. Some of the top companies for recruitment for MBA / PGDM in Marketing include ITC, Marico, Amazon, Flipkart, Godrej, P&G, Coca Cola, Pidilite, Asian Paints, Perfetti, Nestle, etc.

 

 

Q: Which are the top entrance exams for MBA / PGDM in Marketing?

Ans: The top entrance exams for MBA in Sale & Marketing are CAT, XAT, SNAP, NMAT, MAT, CMAT, and GMAT.
