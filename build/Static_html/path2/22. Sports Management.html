MBA in Sports Management

MBA in Sports Management lays out an opportunity for candidates who have a keen interest in the field of sports to explore it from the business point of view. The course exposes candidates to the latest trends and happenings in the field of sports.

MBA Sport Management courses have high job opportunities. Candidates are employed in the sports field concerned with planning, organization, administration, and budgeting. The sector also offers memorable opportunities and is not restricted to a single geographical area, as sports are prevalent in all areas. The job scope for MBA Sports Management is immense. One can make a career as a sports consultant, teacher, venue operator, etc.

FAQs – MBA in Oil & Gas Management

 

Q. What is the scope of MBA in Sports Management?

Ans: MBA Sport Management courses have high job opportunities with both rewarding and full of glitz and glamour, Candidates are employed in the sports field concerned with planning, organization, administration, and budgeting. The sector also offers memorable opportunities and is not restricted to a single geographical area, as sports are prevalent in all areas.

 

 

Q. What is the MBA Sports Management duration in India?

Ans. MBA / PGDM in Sports Management is a 2 year full time post graduation program. However, students can also pursue it in Online mode with a maximum duration of 3 years.

 

Q. What is the eligibility criterion for MBA in Sports Management?

Ans. For MBA / PGDM in Sports Management, a student needs to a have valid degree in any discipline with minimum 50 per cent marks. For reserved category students the marks is 45 per cent.

 

Q. How is admission to MBA Sports Management offered?

Ans: Student has to clear the cut of the entrance exams such as CAT, XAT, MAT, CMAT, and GMAT. Followed by entrance exam, a student has to appear for Group Discussion and Personal Interview for selection round by the college.

 

Q. What is the average fee required to study MBA Sports Management ?

Ans. Fees is totally dependent upon the college and also upon the college being a government college or a private college. Fees range for MBA in Sports Management program is Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 15 Lac.

 

Q. What are the top MBA in Sports Management recruiting areas and companies?

Ans: MBA in Sports Management graduate students have job opportunities in sports organizations, sports academies, sports events, sports leagues, sports product industries, and much more. Some of the major recruiting companies are IMG, GroupM, Rhiti Sports, Edusports, Star Sports, ESPN Cricinfo, JSW Sports, ITW Consulting Pvt Ltd, IOS Sports and Entertainment, Decathlon Sports India Pvt Ltd, etc.

 

Q. What is the salary of a fresher from MBA in Sports Management?

Ans. A fresher after completing MBA / PGDM in Sports Management earns on an average Rs 4 – 5 Lac per annum. This can increase multifold with work experience and industry knowledge.

 

Q. Which are the best colleges to pursue MBA in Sports Management?

Ans: Top colleges offering MBA / PGDM in Sports Management are IIM, Rohtak, K J Somaiya Institute of Management, Mumbai, MIT School of Management, Pune, ISBR Business School, Bangalore, Jain University, Bangalore..
