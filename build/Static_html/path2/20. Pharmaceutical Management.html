MBA in Pharmaceutical Management

MBA in Pharmaceutical Management is ideal for those interested in learning the management and business side of the pharmaceutical and healthcare industry in India. It involves efficient management of several domains such as branding, sales management, financial planning, etc. related to pharmaceutical and healthcare industry.

An MBA in Pharmaceutical Management graduate role is central to ensuring the safe, timely, and well-resourced management of pharma products and market and bringing up several developments within the management to enhance revenue by increasing the branding.

FAQs – MBA in Pharmaceutical Management

 

Q: What is Pharmaceutical Management?

Ans: Pharmaceutical management is the study of pharmaceutical sciences along with the study of marketing and management. It develops skills in planning & operating management techniques, acquiring consultancy skills and solving management problems.

 

Q. What is the eligibility criterion to pursue MBA in Pharmaceutical Management?

Ans: The eligibility criterion for MBA / PGDM in Pharmaceutical Management is graduation or equivalent from a recognised university in an discipline. Most of the institutes have minimum score criteria of 50 per cent in graduation (45 per cent for reserved category).

 

Q. How much does it cost to pursue an MBA in Pharmaceutical Management degree program?

Ans: MBA / PGDM in Pharmaceutical Management fees range from Rs. 1 Lac to Rs. 10 Lac.

 

Q. Which are some of the most popular colleges offering MBA in Pharmaceutical Management?

Ans: Some of the top colleges offering MBA / PGDM in Pharmaceutical Management are UPES, Dehradun, Sharda University, Greater Noida, Chitkara University, Mohali, BML Munjal University, Gurgaon, (more names to be added as per our choice).  To view list of colleges offering MBA / PGDM in Pharmaceutical Management, click here.

 

Q. What are the various job positions available to a fresh MBA in Pharmaceutical Management postgraduate?

Ans: MBA in Pharmaceutical Management comes with various jobs profiles such as Business Development Manager, Market Researcher and Drug Developer , Sales Manager , Drug Distribution Manager, Pharmaceutical Purchase Manager, etc.

 

Q. What salary can a fresher in MBA in Pharmaceutical Management graduate expect?

Ans: A fresher in MBA / PGDM in Pharmaceutical Management can earn a salry of Rs. 4 – 5 Lac per annum. With experience and knowledge a candidate can double the salary in 3 to 4 years.

 

Q. Can I pursue an MBA in Pharmaceutical Management through distance learning mode?

Ans: Yes, MBA in Pharmaceutical Management can be pursued through distance mode of learning.

 

Q: Which are the top entrance exams for MBA / PGDM in Pharmaceutical Management?

Ans: The top entrance exams for MBA / PGDM in Pharmaceutical Management are CAT, XAT, MAT, CMAT, and GMAT.

 

Q: What is the difference between MBA in Pharmaceutical Management and MBA in Hospitality?

Ans: MBA in Pharmaceutical Management aims at producing skilled professionals for pharmaceutical industry. The course imparts skills related to business development, sales and marketing, consultancy and planning and operation management related to pharmaceutical industry. MBA in Hospitality Management course aims at preparing professionals for performing tasks in the hospitality industry. It not only trains them for daily operations in a hotel/restaurant, but also it trains the candidates in handling the accounts, interacting with clients, and handling and growing the business properly.
