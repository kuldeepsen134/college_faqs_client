MBA in Entrepreneurship

MBA in Entrepreneurship is not about making a candidate placement ready, but deals with equipping graduates to start their own business, write a real-world business plan, and run a business internationally. The MBA in Entrepreneurship’s main aim is to cover many facets of business management and teach students how to turn concepts into profitable companies.

MBA / PGDM in Entrepreneurship include business management and teach students how to turn concepts into profitable companies. The Entrepreneurship major equips students with analytical resources, insights, and experiences that train graduates as self-employed entrepreneurs, family-business entrepreneurs, or corporate entrepreneurs.

MBA in Entrepreneurship: Skills Required

MBA / PGDM in Entrepreneurship wants a student to be self reliant and understand how a business can create jobs. General skillsets required for such candidates are:

1.       Understanding Strategy and embracing opportunities

2.       Ability to take the risk

3.       Self-discipline and Integrity

4.       Decisive and action-oriented

5.       Innovation

FAQs – MBA in Entrepreneurship

 

Q: What is an MBA in Entrepreneurship Management?

Ans: MBA / PGDM in Entrepreneurship Management is a degree which deals with every aspect of entrepreneurship, family business handling and start ups.

 

Q: What is the eligibility criterion for MBA in Entrepreneurship Management?

Ans: The eligibility criterion for MBA / PGDM in Entrepreneurship Management is graduation or equivalent from a recognised university in any discipline. Most of the institutes have minimum score criteria of 50 per cent in graduation (45 per cent for reserved category).

 

Q: Which entrance exam score is required for admission to MBA in Entrepreneurship Management?

Ans: For admission to MBA in Information Technology courses offered by colleges in India, candidates can appear for CAT, GMAT, XAT, CMAT and MAT.

 

Q: What is the scope of MBA in Entrepreneurship Management?

Ans: The scope of MBA in entrepreneurship management is huge and there are various employment opportunities available after the completion of graduation.

 

Q: Which companies hire MBA in Entrepreneurship Management graduates?

Ans: TCS, Wipro, Amazon, Google, Flipkart, Infosys, WNS Global Services, Accenture are some of the top companies hiring MBA / PGDM in Entrepreneurship management graduates.
